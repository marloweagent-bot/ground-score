## Additional Notes

### Insomniac Events Dominance
Insomniac (Live Nation) produces many of the top festivals: EDC (LV, Orlando), Beyond Wonderland, Escape Halloween, Countdown NYE, Dreamstate, Nocturnal Wonderland, and partners with Forbidden Kingdom. Their production quality sets the industry standard.

### Regional Distribution
- **California**: EDC LV (nearby), Coachella, Beyond Wonderland, HARD Summer, Lightning in a Bottle, Nocturnal Wonderland, Countdown, Dreamstate, CRSSD
- **Florida**: Ultra, EDC Orlando, Sunset, Forbidden Kingdom, Hulaween
- **Midwest**: Electric Forest (MI), Movement (MI), Lost Lands (OH), Lollapalooza (IL), ARC (IL), Dancefestopia (KS)
- **Pacific Northwest**: Bass Canyon (WA)
- **Texas**: Ubbi Dubbi
- **Utah**: Das Energi
- **Tennessee**: Bonnaroo

### Emerging Trends (2024-2026)
- **Bass music festivals** gaining ground (Lost Lands, Bass Canyon, Forbidden Kingdom)
- **House/techno purist events** growing (ARC, Movement, CRSSD)
- **Boutique transformational festivals** maintaining loyal followings (Electric Forest, Lightning in a Bottle)
