The Stages

Genre-specific programming across seven areas.

  • Main Stage: Headliners across genres. The big names.
  • La Petite Maison du Peuple: Serious electronic music. Techno, house, bass.
  • The Last Arena: Metal and heavier sounds.
  • Dance Hall: Hip-hop, R&B, urban music.
  • Boombox: Alternative and indie.

Survival Guide

Five days requires preparation.

  • Waterproof everything: Belgian summer rain is real.
  • Rubber boots: The mud is legendary.
  • Pace yourself: It's a marathon, not a sprint.
  • Camping supplies: Basic breakfast supplies save money.
  • Sleep strategy: You won't sleep much. Accept it.
