What Is Anjunadeep?

Anjunadeep is the deeper, more introspective sister label to Anjunabeats, both founded by Above & Beyond. While Anjunabeats is trance and euphoria, Anjunadeep is:

  • Deep house: Warm, groovy, soulful
  • Progressive house: Building journeys, emotional peaks
  • Melodic techno: Driving yet cerebral
  • Organic house: Acoustic elements, world music influences

Key artists include Lane 8, Yotto, Ben B√∂hmer, Marsh, Tinlicker, Jody Wisternoff, and dozens more. If you don't know these names, start with Lane 8's "This Never Happened" or Ben B√∂hmer's "Breathing."

US Tour Locations

Open Air events happen at carefully selected outdoor venues:

  • Red Rocks (Colorado): The flagship. Natural amphitheater carved into mountain rocks. Usually November, but check dates. This is the bucket-list show.
  • Brooklyn (NYC): Various outdoor spaces. Great for East Coast fans. Urban but scenic.
  • Washington DC: Recent additions like A.i (The Great Lawn). Spring/summer dates.
  • Los Angeles: Periodic shows at outdoor venues.
  • Global: London, Hong Kong, Barcelona ‚Äî the label tours worldwide.

Before You Go

  • Tickets sell fast: Anjuna family is dedicated. Sign up for label emails for presale access.
  • Know the sound: Don't expect bangers. This is journey music ‚Äî builds, peaks, emotions.
  • Dress in layers: Outdoor shows mean temperature swings. Red Rocks gets cold at night.
  • Red Rocks altitude: 6,450 feet. Hydrate extra, take it easy on alcohol, expect to feel winded.

The Show Experience

Single-stage, curated journey:

  • Format: Back-to-back sets, usually 4-6 artists over 6-10 hours
  • Sound: Impeccable. The label doesn't compromise on audio quality.
  • Transitions: Often seamless between artists. The whole night flows as one experience.
  • Phones: Lane 8's "This Never Happened" ethos influences the culture ‚Äî many put phones away.
