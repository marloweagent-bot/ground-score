What's an "Ubbi Dubbi"?

Ubbi Dubbi is a language game where you insert "ub" before every vowel sound. "Hello" becomes "Hubelluboh." It's silly, nonsensical, and perfectly captures the playful spirit Disco Donnie wanted for the festival.

The name represents a fresh start from Something Wonderful ‚Äî new branding, new venue, new vibe. No baggage from 2015's Cotton Bowl disaster.

The Venue: Panther Island

Panther Island Pavilion sits along the Trinity River in Fort Worth:

  • Location: Urban-adjacent but feels removed from city bustle
  • Waterfront: Trinity River adds some atmosphere
  • Layout issues: Narrow paths to main stage create bottlenecks
  • Camping: On-site camping across the river from stages
"The only annoying thing is that the main stage is a long-ass narrow path from the rest of the festival which tends to get crowded since everyone gets funneled in there." ‚Äî Reddit r/aves, 2024

Camping at Ubbi Dubbi

The biggest upgrade from Something Wonderful ‚Äî actual camping:

  • General Camping: Basic plots, bring your tent, community forms
  • Premium Camping: Better location, extra amenities
  • Arrival: Check-in starts Friday (or Thursday for early birds)
  • Experience: Pre-parties, late-night hangouts, extended community

If you're doing Ubbi Dubbi, camping is the move. Day passes work, but you miss the community aspect.

Survival Tips

Memorial Day in Texas means HEAT. Prepare accordingly:

  • Hydration: Drink constantly. Bring electrolyte packets.
  • Shade: Canopy for your campsite is essential
  • Timing: Take breaks during peak afternoon heat (3-6 PM)
  • Clothing: Breathable fabrics, hat, sunscreen
  • Coolers: Ice runs available; keep food/drinks cold
  • Portable fans: Battery-powered fans are lifesavers in tents

Known Issues

Ubbi Dubbi has improved but still has challenges:

  • Venue layout: Main stage access path creates congestion
  • Parking: 2022 was reportedly a "shitshow" ‚Äî improved since but watch announcements
  • Heat: Not the festival's fault, but Texas Memorial Day is brutal
  • Scale: Venue may be at capacity limit; growth unclear

Fort Worth Extras

Make a long weekend of it:

  • Friday pre-parties: Sugarmans and other venues host DJs
  • Fort Worth Stockyards: Texas culture, honky-tonks, BBQ
  • Food scene: Dallas-Fort Worth has excellent restaurants
  • Recovery Monday: Memorial Day off = built-in rest day

Honest Assessment

Ubbi Dubbi is a solid regional festival ‚Äî not trying to be EDC, just serving Texas. If you go in with appropriate expectations:

  • Pros: Camping option, friendly crowd, diverse lineup, affordable Texas trip
  • Cons: Venue logistics, extreme heat, production not tier-1
  • Verdict: Worth it for Texas locals and regional fans. Not a destination fest for out-of-staters unless camping fests are your thing.
