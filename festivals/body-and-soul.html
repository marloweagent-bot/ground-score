Getting There

  • Location: Ballinlough Castle Estate, County Westmeath.
  • From Dublin: About 90 minutes drive. Shuttle buses available.
  • Driving: Most people drive. Parking included in ticket.
  • Flying: Dublin Airport, then shuttle or rental car.

The Experience

  • The Woodlands: Secret stages and immersive experiences in the forest.
  • The Midnight Garden: Electronic music in a walled garden setting.
  • The Castle: The medieval backdrop for special performances.
  • The Lakes: Wild swimming and lakeside stages.
  • Healing Gardens: Wellness, yoga, and restoration spaces.

Camping & Accommodation

  • General Camping: Included in ticket. Bring your own tent.
  • Boutique Camping: Bell tents, tipis, and glamping options.
  • Campervan: Dedicated areas for vehicles.
  • Hot Tubs: Yes, there are communal hot tubs. Yes, they're amazing.

Survival Tips

  • Book early: Body & Soul sells out. Don't wait.
  • Pack wellies: Irish weather. Be prepared for mud.
  • Embrace wellness: Try a yoga class or healing session ‚Äî it's part of the experience.
  • Explore: The hidden stages and secret spots reward wandering.
  • Late nights: Music continues into the small hours. Pace yourself.
