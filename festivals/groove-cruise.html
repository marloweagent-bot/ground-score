üé´ Cabin Booking System

Groove Cruise uses cabin bookings, not traditional tickets. Prices are per person based on cabin type (interior, ocean view, balcony, suite) and occupancy. Payment plans available. Book early ‚Äî cabins sell out and prices increase. Gratuities and drink packages are additional.

Before You Go

A cruise festival requires different prep than a land festival.

  • Book cabins early: Best prices and cabin selection go fast. Payment plans help spread the cost.
  • Cruise documents: US citizens need passport or birth certificate + government ID. Check requirements.
  • Drink package: Optional but recommended. All-you-can-drink for a flat rate. Worth it for most.
  • Theme party prep: 8 themes mean 8 outfits. Start planning costumes early. The community goes hard.
  • Artist experiences: Sign up for hosted activities once booking opens. Popular ones fill up.

The Ship: Allure of the Seas

One of the largest cruise ships ever built becomes a floating festival grounds.

  • Two-deck dance hall: Main stage production with massive LED walls and sound.
  • Pool deck: Outdoor stages with ocean views. Sunrise and sunset sets hit different here.
  • Theater: Seated shows and intimate performances in a proper concert hall.
  • Lounges & bars: Pop-up stages in unexpected spots. 19 bars total.
  • Amenities: Ice skating, surf simulator, zip line, spa, pools, rock climbing. It's a cruise.
