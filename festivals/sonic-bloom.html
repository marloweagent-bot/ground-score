Current Status

Sonic Bloom took 2024 off and the future is uncertain. Reports suggest permit issues with local authorities and financial challenges from low ticket sales contributed to the hiatus. The 18-year-old festival has weathered challenges before, but the current situation requires patience from the community.

If you're hoping to attend, follow official channels and be prepared for uncertainty. The community remains hopeful but realistic about a return.

History

Sonic Bloom has evolved significantly since 2006:

  • 2006: Founded at Mishawaka Amphitheater near Fort Collins as a 2-day event
  • Evolution: Grew into 4-day immersive camping festival
  • Hummingbird Ranch: Found its home in the Spanish Peaks foothills
  • 2017: Collaborated on Oregon Eclipse with Symbiosis
  • Present: On hiatus after permit and financial challenges

The Philosophy

Sonic Bloom was founded on "Universal Field Theory" ‚Äî the idea that everything and everyone is universally connected:

  • Sustainability: Environmental consciousness built into operations
  • Transformation: Personal and community growth as central values
  • Unification: Breaking down barriers between people and genres
  • Fun: Joy and celebration as spiritual practice
"Sonic Bloom's focus has always been sustainability, transformation, unification, and fun in a beautiful natural setting. The lineups are full of local and world-renowned electronic acts with two nights going until sunrise." ‚Äî Festival Description

The Setting

Hummingbird Ranch in the Spanish Peaks foothills offers some of the best festival scenery in America:

  • Elevation: High-altitude Colorado mountain setting
  • Views: Spanish Peaks visible throughout the grounds
  • Weather: June brings warm days and cool nights
  • Sunrises: Mountain dawn is worth staying awake for

Getting There

Hummingbird Ranch is remote by design ‚Äî southwest of Pueblo in Spanish Peaks country:

  • From Denver: 3+ hour drive south
  • From Colorado Springs: 2+ hours
  • Airports: Denver (DEN) or Colorado Springs (COS)
  • Carpools: Colorado's festival community organizes rideshares

The Music

Sonic Bloom's curation emphasizes thoughtful electronic and live hybrid acts:

  • Bass & Experimental: CloZee, KOAN Sound, Alix Perez, A Hundred Drums
  • Live Electronic: Lotus, Papadosio, SunSquabi, Dirtwire
  • Sunrise Sets: Two nights of music until dawn
  • Discovery: Local and rising talent alongside headliners

Workshops & Growth

Programming extends far beyond music:

  • Yoga: Daily practice in mountain settings
  • Meditation: Guided and open sessions
  • Flow Arts: Workshops and late-night spinning communities
  • Sustainability: Education on environmental practice
  • Personal Growth: Various modalities and approaches
