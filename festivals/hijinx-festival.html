Before You Go

December in Philly ‚Äî plan accordingly.

  • Book hotels NOW: Holiday rates in Center City are steep. Book months ahead or look just outside downtown.
  • Layer up outside: Freezing to/from venue, furnace inside. Coat check is your friend.
  • Earplugs essential: Indoor bass hits different. Protect your hearing.
  • Holiday recovery: You're doing this between Christmas and NYE. Pace yourself.

Getting There & Getting In

Pennsylvania Convention Center is as accessible as venues get.

  • Public transit: SEPTA subway and regional rail connect directly. No Uber surge needed.
  • Amtrak: 30th Street Station is close. NYC, DC, Baltimore ‚Äî easy train ride.
  • Airport: PHL is 20 minutes on SEPTA. Direct rail connection.
  • Entry: Lines can be long first day. VIP gets faster entry.

The Festival Grounds

Industrial convention space transformed into bass cathedral.

  • Main Hall: Massive room with the biggest acts. Get lost in the production.
  • Side Stages: Smaller rooms with different vibes. Less crowded, more intimate.
  • Sound: Indoor acoustics mean bass HITS. Feel it in your chest.
  • Production: Arena-level lights, lasers, LED. This isn't a warehouse party.
"When Excision dropped that first bass hit and the entire floor shook, I knew HiJinx was different." ‚Äî Philly local, 2023

Food, Water & Survival

Convention center limitations, but manageable.

  • Inside food: Limited and expensive. Think arena concessions.
  • Eat beforehand: Center City has incredible restaurants. Fuel up properly.
  • Water: Refill stations available. Stay hydrated despite the cold outside.
  • Coat check: USE IT. You'll be sweating in minutes.

The Crowd & Community

Bass fam celebrating the holidays together.

  • Demographics: 18-30 primarily. College students on winter break strong.
  • Energy: Mosh pits, headbanging, intense. People go HARD.
  • Holiday spirit: Festive costumes mix with rave gear. Santa hats and kandi.
  • Regional mix: NYC, DC, Jersey, local Philly all represented.

After the Festival

Philly doesn't sleep, especially between holidays.

  • Afterparties: Local clubs host late-night events. Check for official announcements.
  • Late-night food: Philly cheesesteaks at 3am hit different post-rave.
  • NYE prep: Two days to recover before the real NYE festivities begin.
  • Checkout: Hotels may have late checkout available ‚Äî ask nicely.
