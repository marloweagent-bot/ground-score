Getting There & Getting In

Pimlico Race Course is in northwest Baltimore. Accessible by car, rideshare, or limited public transit.

  • Driving: Parking lots available, arrive early
  • Rideshare: Designated pickup/dropoff, expect surge pricing
  • Transit: Limited options‚Äîcar or rideshare recommended
  • Entry: Can be slow‚Äîarrive early to avoid peak lines

The Festival Grounds

The historic racetrack provides a large, flat venue with multiple stage areas. Grandstands offer some shade and elevated views. The infield and track areas host the main stages.

The Crowd & Community

Mix of DMV (DC/Maryland/Virginia) scene with Philly and Virginia contingents. Generally friendly but can skew young. Bass stages tend to have more experienced festival-goers. Local community is supportive of the festival.

After the Festival

Baltimore's Inner Harbor has nightlife options. Recovery day might involve Baltimore crab cakes and air conditioning. The city is worth exploring if you have extra time.
