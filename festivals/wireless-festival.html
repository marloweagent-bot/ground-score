Getting There

  • Tube: Finsbury Park (Victoria & Piccadilly lines) ‚Äî main entrance nearby.
  • Overground: Manor House (Piccadilly line) as alternative.
  • Buses: 4, 19, 106, 153, 236, 253, 254, 259 all serve Finsbury Park.
  • Driving: Limited parking. Public transport strongly recommended.

What to Wear

Wireless is a fashion event as much as a music festival:

  • Streetwear: Designer brands, fresh kicks, and current trends dominate.
  • Comfortable footwear: You'll be standing for hours. Balance style with function.
  • Layers: July days can be hot, evenings cooler.
  • Rain backup: British summer. Bring something waterproof just in case.

Survival Tips

  • Arrive early: Get positioned for headline sets. Main stage gets packed.
  • Battery pack: Your phone will die. Video and photos drain batteries fast.
  • Meetup plan: Signal can be patchy. Agree meeting points with friends beforehand.
  • Stay hydrated: Water points are free. Don't rely only on alcohol.
  • Check clash times: Plan your day. Headline acts often overlap.

After The Festival

  • Local area: Finsbury Park has bars and restaurants nearby.
  • Night tube: Victoria and Piccadilly lines run until late on weekends.
