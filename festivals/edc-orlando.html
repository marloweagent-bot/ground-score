Before You Go

EDC Orlando closes festival season with perfect November weather.

  • November timing is ideal: Florida humidity is gone, temps are 65-75¬∞F. Best weather of any major EDM festival.
  • Book hotels early: Orlando has infinite hotels but EDC weekend prices spike. International Drive area is popular.
  • Plan your stages: 8+ stages means schedule conflicts. Use the app and prioritize your must-sees.
  • Layer for night: November evenings can drop to 55-60¬∞F. Bring a light jacket or hoodie.

Getting There & Getting In

Orlando is one of the most accessible festival cities in America.

  • MCO airport: Major hub with cheap flights from everywhere. Spirit, Frontier, JetBlue all serve it heavily.
  • Hotels: Thousands of options‚ÄîInternational Drive, Downtown, Disney area. All within 15-30 min of Tinker Field.
  • Shuttles: Insomniac runs shuttles from major hotel zones. Worth it for no parking hassle.
  • Rideshare: Works fine. Post-festival surge is real but not as bad as Vegas.
  • Entry: Standard security with multiple gates. Arrive early to avoid the rush.

The Festival Grounds

Tinker Field transforms into a full EDC wonderland.

  • kineticFIELD: The iconic main stage with animated structures, massive production. Headliners and fireworks here.
  • circuitGROUNDS: Second main stage with equally impressive design. Often has the best set time conflicts.
  • neonGARDEN: House and techno haven. More intimate, dedicated community.
  • bassPOD: Where the bass heads gather. Dubstep, riddim, heavy stuff.
  • Additional stages: wasteLAND (hardstyle), stereoBLOOM, and discovery stages round out the experience.

Food, Water & Survival

November weather makes survival much easier than summer festivals.

  • Weather advantage: No heat exhaustion, no dust storms, no monsoons. Just comfortable dancing weather.
  • Water: Free fill stations throughout. Stay hydrated but you won't be sweating like Vegas May.
  • Food: Standard EDC vendors. Eat before peak hours or during less popular sets.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll walk a lot but Tinker Field is more compact than the Speedway.

The Crowd & Community

EDC Orlando draws a mix of locals, East Coast pilgrims, and Vegas veterans.

  • Florida ravers: Strong local scene with experienced festival-goers.
  • East Coast representation: Georgia, Carolina, Virginia crews make the trip.
  • Friendlier scale: 80,000 feels manageable compared to Vegas 170,000. More room to breathe.
  • PLUR culture: All EDC events maintain that community feeling. Kandi, kindness, connection.

After the Festival

Orlando has plenty to offer before and after EDC.

  • Theme parks: You're in Orlando. Disney, Universal, and more if you want to extend your trip.
  • Club scene: Downtown Orlando has afterparties and clubs for those with energy left.
  • Recovery day: Build in time before flying home. Pool day at the hotel or theme park adventure.
  • Festival season closer: EDC Orlando traditionally ends the major festival calendar. Go big.
