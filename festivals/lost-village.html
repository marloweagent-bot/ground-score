The World

Lost Village's "abandoned village" concept is fully realized:

  • The Abandoned Chapel: Comedy, talks, and late-night feasting in a ruined church.
  • The Junkyard: Industrial wasteland turned techno temple.
  • The Forgotten Cinema: Open-air movies under the stars.
  • Various woodland stages: Emerge from the trees like fever dreams.

Getting There

  • Location: Secret forest in Lincolnshire, revealed closer to the event.
  • Driving: Most attendees drive. Parking included.
  • Coach: Official coaches from London, Manchester, Birmingham, and more.
  • Train: Nearest station is Newark North Gate, then taxi/shuttle.

Camping Options

  • General Camping: Included in ticket. Bring your own tent.
  • Boutique Camping: Pre-pitched tents and bell tents available.
  • The Lodge: Premium accommodation with beds and breakfast.
  • Campervan: Dedicated area for vehicles.

Survival Tips

  • Embrace the world: The more you engage with the concept, the better your experience.
  • Book dining experiences: The secret dinners sell out fast. Plan ahead.
  • Explore early: The site reveals its secrets to those who wander.
  • Dress for the weather: Forest nights can be cool and damp. Layers essential.
  • Pace the food: You'll want to eat everything. Budget accordingly.
