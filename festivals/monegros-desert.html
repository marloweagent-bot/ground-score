Survival Essentials

This is not optional advice. This is how you don't die.

  • Water: Bring more than you think. At least 5+ liters per person. Refill stations exist but lines are long.
  • Sun protection: Desert sun is brutal. Hat, sunscreen, long sleeves for day.
  • Layers: 40¬∞C by day, 15¬∞C at night. Plan for both extremes.
  • Dust protection: Bandana or mask for the dust storms. Goggles help.
  • Footwear: Closed shoes essential. The ground is rough.

Getting There

The desert is remote by design.

  • Bus packages: Official buses from Barcelona, Madrid, Bilbao, Valencia. Book early.
  • Car: Parking areas available but expect long walks. Carpool recommended.
  • Nearest airport: Zaragoza (ZAZ) or Barcelona (BCN). Zaragoza is closer.
  • Arrive early: Setup camping before the heat peaks.

The 24-Hour Strategy

Pacing is everything when the music doesn't stop.

  • Rest before: Come rested. You won't sleep during the event.
  • Shade breaks: Take breaks during peak heat (12-5pm). Find shade, hydrate.
  • Peak hours: Sunset to sunrise is when the energy peaks. Save yourself for this.
  • The sunrise set: The iconic Monegros moment. Don't miss it.

Camping Reality

Basic camping is included. Set expectations accordingly.

  • Bring everything: Tent, sleeping gear, shade structure, cooking supplies.
  • Shade is gold: A tarp or shade structure is essential for day rest.
  • Lock valuables: Car is your locker. Don't leave anything unattended.
  • Community: Make friends with neighbors. The shared struggle bonds people.
