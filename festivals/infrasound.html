Before You Go

Infrasound rewards preparation but doesn't punish spontaneity.

  • Show up early: Thursday arrival gets you the best camping spots in the trees.
  • Bring earplugs: Sounds counterintuitive but the systems are LOUD. Protect your hearing to enjoy all four days.
  • Know the lineup: This isn't mainstream ‚Äî research artists beforehand to plan your must-sees.
  • May in Minnesota: Weather is usually pleasant but pack layers for cool nights.

Getting There & Getting In

Harmony Park is in Clarks Grove, Minnesota ‚Äî rural but accessible.

  • From Minneapolis: ~90 minutes south. Easy day-of drive.
  • From Chicago: ~6 hours. Worth the drive.
  • Airport: MSP (Minneapolis-St. Paul) is closest for fly-ins.
  • Entry: No aggressive security. No power-tripping staff. Smooth and welcoming.

The Festival Grounds

Three stages, three world-class sound systems, one mission: audio perfection.

  • Main Stage (Funktion-One): The crown jewel. Headliners and the deepest bass you'll ever feel.
  • Second Stage (Element 5): Different sonic character, equally impressive.
  • Third Stage (Void): Smaller but no less powerful. Late-night home.
  • Layout: Compact and easy. Nothing is far from anything else.

Food, Water & Survival

Straightforward logistics in a forgiving environment.

  • Water: Stations available, but bring your own hydration pack.
  • Food: Multiple vendors, reasonable prices, solid variety.
  • Special mention: kLL sMTH's "gRLL sMTH" live cooking/DJ set is legendary.
  • Camp cooking: Bring your own setup if you want to save money.
