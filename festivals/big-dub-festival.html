Before You Go

Five days requires proper preparation.

  • Camping essentials: Tent, sleeping bag, camp chairs, cooking supplies. This is real camping.
  • Food supplies: Bring groceries for 5 days. Coolers, ice, and easy-prep meals.
  • Shade: July in PA can be hot. Bring a canopy or tarp for daytime shade.
  • Swimsuit: Daily swim parties at the pond are essential for staying cool.
  • Community mindset: Come ready to share, connect, and be part of something.

Getting There

Rural location requires planning.

  • Driving: Best option. 2 hours from DC, 3 from Philly, 4 from NYC.
  • Carpools: Check the Big Dub Facebook group for ride shares.
  • Flying: BWI or DCA, then rent a car. No public transit options.
  • Arrive Wednesday: Give yourself time to set up camp before music starts.

The Festival Grounds

Four Quarters Farm is 300 acres of magic.

  • Four stages: Each with distinct sound and style. Main stage, bass stage, house stage, late-night.
  • The pond: Daily swim parties. Essential for July heat. Bring a floatie.
  • Decorations: Community-built installations throughout. DIY aesthetic done right.
  • Secret sets: Headliner sets happen at random times in random places. Stay alert.
