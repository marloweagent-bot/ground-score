Getting There

  • Shuttle Bus: Runs from Bristol city center. Highly recommended.
  • Driving: Limited parking available. Book in advance.
  • Train: Bristol Temple Meads, then shuttle or taxi.
  • Walking: Not practical from city center ‚Äî about 3 miles.

The Stages

  • Main Stage: Headliners and major acts.
  • DnB Arena: Drum & bass focused all weekend.
  • The Lonely Hearts Club: House, disco, and feel-good vibes.
  • Brouhaha: Techno and heavier sounds.
  • Various other stages: Genre-specific showcases.

Survival Tips

  • Arrive early: Entry queues can build. Get there when gates open.
  • Comfortable shoes: The site is hilly. Good footwear matters.
  • Layers: May can be unpredictable. Evening gets cooler.
  • Battery pack: Your phone will die. Plan meeting points with friends.
  • Afterparties: Bristol's clubs continue the party. Check listings.

Staying in Bristol

No camping at the festival, so you'll need accommodation:

  • Book early: Bank holiday weekend fills up fast.
  • Central Bristol: Best for nightlife and transport links.
  • Clifton: Closer to Ashton Court, lovely area.
  • Budget options: Hostels around Stokes Croft and Old City.
