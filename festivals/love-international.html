The Legacy

Love International was born from The Garden Festival, which ran from 2006-2015 and defined the Croatian boutique festival experience. When Garden ended, Love International continued the ethos: small scale, exceptional curation, music discovery over spectacle.

The founders ‚Äî the same team behind London's Brilliant Corners and the revered Mr. Scruff ‚Äî bring decades of music knowledge to programming. This isn't corporate festival booking; it's a passion project.
