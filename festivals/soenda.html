Getting There

Utrecht makes it easy.

  • Bike: 15 minutes from city center. Bike parking at the festival.
  • Bus: Shuttle from Utrecht Centraal.
  • From Amsterdam: 30 min train to Utrecht, then shuttle/bike.
  • Car: Limited parking. Use public transport if possible.

Winter Events

Soenda runs indoor events December through March.

  • Soenda Presents: Club events at Gietijzer Utrecht.
  • NYE Special: December 26, 27, 31 events.
  • Quality maintained: Same curation, industrial indoor setting.
