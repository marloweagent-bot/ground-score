Before You Go

Get Freaky is about Halloween ‚Äî costume planning is essential.

  • Costume early: The crowd here takes costumes seriously. Plan ahead and bring something you can dance in.
  • Layers: October in Utah can be cold at night. Bring something warm for between sets.
  • Book downtown SLC: Hotels are walkable to good food and the fairpark is accessible.
  • Utah alcohol: 5% ABV limit on beer. Plan accordingly.

The Festival Grounds

Utah State Fairpark transforms into a Halloween wonderland.

  • Multiple stages: Main stage plus tented stages with different vibes.
  • Halloween decor: Spooky theming throughout ‚Äî they commit to the aesthetic.
  • Indoor/outdoor: Mix of covered and open areas handles weather well.
  • Compact enough: Easy to explore without missing sets.
