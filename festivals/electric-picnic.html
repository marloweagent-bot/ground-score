The Areas

  • Main Stage: The headliners. Massive production and capacity.
  • Rankin's Wood: Intimate performances in forest setting.
  • Salty Dog Stage: Electronic music and late-night hedonism.
  • Mindfield: Ideas, debate, and conversations that matter.
  • Jerry Fish Electric Sideshow: Carnival-esque immersive world.
  • Body & Soul: The wellness and healing area.

Getting There

  • Location: Stradbally Hall, County Laois ‚Äî about 90 minutes from Dublin.
  • Shuttle Buses: Run from Dublin, Cork, Galway, and other cities.
  • Driving: Most people drive. Traffic management improved over the years.
  • Flying: Dublin Airport, then shuttle or rental car.

Tickets

  • Loyalty System: Previous attendees get priority access.
  • Sells Out: Usually within days of going on sale. Be ready.
  • Family Camping: Dedicated quiet area for families with children.
  • Boutique Options: Glamping and premium camping available.

Survival Tips

  • Wellies essential: Irish weather. Even in August. Bring them.
  • Explore early: So much to discover. Don't spend all weekend at Main Stage.
  • Late nights: Salty Dog and other areas run late. Pace yourself.
  • Loyals sale: If you go, register for next year's loyalty sale immediately.
  • Embrace the craic: Talk to strangers. This is Ireland. Everyone's friendly.
