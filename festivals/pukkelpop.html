The Stages

Each with its own musical identity.

  • Main Stage: The headliners. Massive production, iconic sets.
  • Marquee: Second stage. Often hosts alternative legends.
  • Boiler Room: Underground electronic. The reason many come.
  • Dance Hall: More accessible electronic and dance music.
  • Club: Late-night programming. Intimate vibes.

Camping Tips

  • Arrive early: Best spots go fast. Thursday arrival recommended.
  • Bring shade: August Belgian sun can be strong.
  • Rain gear: Weather changes. Be prepared.
  • Earplugs for sleeping: The camping area has its own parties.
