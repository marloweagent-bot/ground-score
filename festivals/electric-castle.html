Before You Go

Electric Castle requires camping commitment but rewards with total immersion.

  • Flights: Fly into Cluj-Napoca (CLJ). Shuttles to festival grounds available.
  • Camping: Various tiers from basic to glamping. Book early for better options.
  • Gear: Bring proper camping equipment ‚Äî nights can be cool even in July.
  • Prepare: Download the app, plan your must-sees, but leave room for discovery.

Getting There & Getting In

More remote than UNTOLD but well-organized logistics.

  • Shuttles: Regular buses from Cluj-Napoca and airport to festival.
  • Entry: RFID wristband gives access to camping and festival grounds.
  • Distance: 30km from Cluj, but once you're there, you're staying.
