Before You Go

Decadence Colorado is an indoor festival in downtown Denver. Come prepared for winter but leave your coat at coat check.

  • Book downtown: Hotels within walking distance eliminate rideshare stress. 16th Street Mall area is ideal.
  • Light rail from DEN: Skip rental cars. The A Line runs from the airport to Union Station (downtown).
  • Layer smart: It's cold outside but hot inside. Use the coat check ‚Äî it's worth it.
  • Two-day commitment: Both nights are essential. Dec 30 is often less crowded with great sets.

Getting There & Getting In

Colorado Convention Center is at 700 14th St in the heart of downtown Denver.

  • Walk: If you're staying downtown, you can walk. Simple as that.
  • Light rail: Convention Center station is a block away.
  • Entry lines: Doors open at 5:30pm, event starts 6:30pm. Arrive early on NYE for smoother entry.
  • Will call: In the convention center lobby. Plan extra time if you need to pick up tickets.

The Festival Grounds

Two massive arenas within the convention center, plus art installations and lounges throughout.

  • Arena 1: Typically main stage / big room EDM. Headliners and the countdown.
  • Arena 2: Usually bass-heavy or house-focused depending on the year.
  • Corridors: Art installations, photo ops, food vendors, and chill spaces between arenas.

Food, Water & Survival

Indoor venue means no weather concerns, but stay hydrated anyway.

  • Water: Refillable stations throughout. Bring an empty bottle.
  • Food: Multiple vendor locations. Standard festival pricing ($15-20).
  • Bars: 21+ with ID. Multiple locations, expect lines during peak times.
  • Pre-game: Downtown Denver has great restaurants within walking distance. Eat before entry.

The Crowd & Community

Colorado's rave scene is strong, and Decadence attracts ravers from across the country.

  • Age range: 18+ entry, crowd peaks 21-30. Mature vibes overall.
  • Colorado locals: Passionate scene that shows up in full force.
  • Tourists: Major destination festival draws from everywhere.
  • Energy: Peak NYE excitement. The countdown moment is genuinely electric.

After the Festival

Downtown Denver keeps the party going.

  • No re-entry: Once you're out, you're out. Plan accordingly.
  • Afterparties: Local venues host official and unofficial afters. Check event listings.
  • Recovery: Jan 1st brunch spots are walking distance. Sleep in, you earned it.
  • Skiing?: Mountains are 1-2 hours away if you want to extend your trip.
