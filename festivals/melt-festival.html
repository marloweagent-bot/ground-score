The Stages

Each stage has its own character amid the industrial landscape.

  • Big Wheel: The main stage with a massive Ferris wheel backdrop. Headliners play here.
  • Sleepless Floor: 24-hour techno bunker. Dark, loud, relentless. Doesn't close.
  • Gemini: Twin stage setup for electronic and indie crossover acts.
  • Medusa: Excavator-integrated stage. Literally built into the mining machinery.
  • Scheune: Barn stage for intimate sets and experimental bookings.

Camping

Camping is part of the experience and included in most tickets.

  • Standard camping: Huge open fields. Bring quality gear ‚Äî nights can be cool.
  • Lake access: Some camping areas near the swimming lake.
  • Glamping: Pre-pitched tent options available with beds.
  • Car camping: Available in designated areas with vehicle access.
