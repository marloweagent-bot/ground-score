What is Ozora?

Ozora is not really comparable to other festivals. It's a week-long gathering of the global psytrance tribe ‚Äî part music festival, part spiritual retreat, part temporary utopia experiment. The music never stops (literally 24/7 for a week), and neither does the deeper exploration.

Originating from the legendary Goa parties of the '90s, Ozora represents the continuation of that psychedelic dance culture. It's a pilgrimage for those who've found meaning in the trance experience.

The Music

  • Main Stage: The iconic dome. Full-on, progressive, goa trance
  • Chill Out: Ambient, downtempo, psychill
  • Pumpui: Harder, darker psytrance
  • Dragon Nest: Forest psy, hitech
  • Artistry: World music, live acts

Artists from across the psytrance spectrum ‚Äî Ace Ventura, Astrix, Raja Ram, Infected Mushroom, and hundreds more.

Beyond Music

  • Workshops: Yoga, meditation, breathwork, dance, healing arts
  • Talks: Philosophy, environmentalism, alternative living
  • Art Village: Creating and exhibiting throughout the week
  • Healing Area: Alternative therapies and integration support
  • The Lake: Swimming and washing off the dust

Preparation

This is not a casual weekend festival. Prepare for:

  • Camping gear: Tent, sleeping setup, shade structure essential
  • Heat: Hungarian summer can be brutal. Shade, water, electrolytes
  • Dust: It gets dusty. Bandanas, goggles if sensitive
  • Self-sufficiency: Bring basics, don't rely entirely on vendors
  • Mental preparation: A week of intensity requires stamina

Getting There

  • Budapest Airport: 90+ minutes to site
  • Festival Buses: Official shuttles from Budapest
  • Driving: Parking available (fills up)
  • Arrive early: Best camping spots go first days
