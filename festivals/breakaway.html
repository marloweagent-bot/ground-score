Before You Go

Breakaway is low-prep compared to camping festivals, but a few things help:

  • Early bird tickets: Significant savings if you buy when announced. Use promo codes from influencers for additional 10% off.
  • Hotel vs. commute: If the venue is central, staying nearby opens up afterparty options.
  • Check the indoor stage schedule: It closes for headliners ‚Äî plan your undercard viewing accordingly.
  • Afterparty tickets: Often sold separately. Levity-hosted events have been highlights in multiple cities.

Getting There & Getting In

Urban venues mean urban logistics ‚Äî generally straightforward.

  • Rideshare: Works well. Surge pricing applies at close but manageable.
  • Parking: Available at most venues. Arrive early for better spots.
  • Entry: Standard security ‚Äî bag checks, metal detectors. Lines can build for headliners but rarely excessive.
  • Re-entry: Check specific city policy, varies by venue.

The Festival Grounds

Compact, efficient, focused on the music:

  • Main Stage: Outdoor, big production, all headliners. Get there early for prime positioning.
  • Indoor/Second Stage: More intimate, often better crowd energy. Closes during main stage headliners.
  • Vendor areas: Standard festival setup ‚Äî food, merch, sponsors.

Food, Water & Survival

  • Water: Free stations available, but lines can form. Bring an empty bottle.
  • Food: Standard festival food trucks. Nothing exceptional but functional.
  • Weather: Varies by city and season. Check local forecasts ‚Äî some cities do spring, others do winter indoor editions.
  • Pro tip: Eat before entering. More options, better prices, shorter waits.

The Crowd & Community

Know what you're getting into:

  • Demographics: Younger crowd (18-28), many first-time festival-goers, mainstream EDM fans.
  • Energy: Concert-crowd energy more than rave-family vibes. Less kandi, more phone flashlights.
  • Friendly: Generally good vibes, just different from Insomniac PLUR culture.
  • Afterparties: Often better, tighter community at the after events.

After the Festival

  • Official afterparties: Check Breakaway and Levity socials for announcements. Separate tickets.
  • Local clubs: Many cities have EDM-friendly clubs that go late.
  • Sunday recovery: No camping means you sleep in a real bed. Brunch exists.
