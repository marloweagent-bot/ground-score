Before You Go

Skyline is a city festival ‚Äî plan accordingly.

  • Book hotels early: LA hotels near Expo Park fill up. Consider Downtown or Koreatown.
  • Plan after-parties: The official and unofficial after-parties are essential. Research ahead.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be on your feet dancing for hours. Prioritize function.
  • Know your artists: The lineup rewards research. Dig into sets beforehand.

Getting There

LA accessibility makes this easy.

  • Metro: Expo Park has direct Metro access. Skip the parking hassle.
  • Rideshare: Uber/Lyft work well. Surge pricing after the fest, but manageable.
  • Driving: Parking available but fills up. Arrive early or use public transit.
  • Fly into LAX: Cheap flights from everywhere, easy airport to anywhere.
