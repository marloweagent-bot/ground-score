Before You Go

Decadence Arizona is a two-night outdoor festival in the desert. December weather is mild but prepare for cool nights.

  • Book early: Phoenix hotels are affordable but fill up for NYE. Downtown or Scottsdale gives you afterparty options.
  • Layer up: Desert temps drop significantly at night (50-60¬∞F). Bring a jacket you're okay losing in the crowd.
  • Two-day tickets: Single day available but the full experience requires both nights.
  • VIP worth it?: Elevated viewing, shorter lines, dedicated bars. Worth considering for NYE night specifically.

Getting There & Getting In

Phoenix Raceway is in Avondale, about 25 miles west of downtown Phoenix.

  • Fly into PHX: Phoenix Sky Harbor is a major hub with cheap flights from everywhere.
  • Drive + Park: On-site parking available. Expect traffic on exit, especially NYE night.
  • Rideshare: Works but surges hard on NYE. Have a backup plan or book in advance.
  • Entry: Standard security checks. Arrive early on NYE to avoid peak entry lines.

The Festival Grounds

Phoenix Raceway transforms into a multi-stage EDM playground with themed production.

  • Main Stage: Headliners, big room, and the midnight countdown. Gets crowded for peak sets.
  • Bass Stage: Dubstep, riddim, experimental bass. The headbanger headquarters.
  • House/Techno Stage: Underground vibes for those escaping the wubs.

Food, Water & Survival

Standard festival logistics in a desert setting.

  • Water: Free refill stations available. Bring an empty bottle or CamelBak.
  • Food: Multiple vendors with typical festival options. Prices are $12-18 for entrees.
  • Weather: December nights are cool ‚Äî way more comfortable than Arizona's reputation suggests.
  • Pro tip: Eat before 10PM. Lines get long as midnight approaches.

The Crowd & Community

Phoenix rave culture is welcoming and enthusiastic.

  • Local love: Strong Arizona rave community that returns every year.
  • Age range: 18+ crowd skews younger (21-28) but all ages welcome.
  • Vibe: High energy, friendly, bass-focused. Kandi trading happens.

After the Festival

Phoenix has options for recovery and continuation.

  • Afterparties: Check local venues in Scottsdale and downtown Phoenix.
  • New Year's Day: Phoenix has great brunch spots for recovery. Pool season starts early here.
  • Flight home: Don't book anything before noon on Jan 1st.
