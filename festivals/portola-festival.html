Before You Go

Portola typically announces in spring for September dates. Tickets sell out fast‚ÄîGA often gone within days. The lineup announcement drives sales since the booking is always strong.

  • Book hotels early‚ÄîSF hotel prices spike during festival weekend
  • Plan layers‚ÄîBay weather is unpredictable, wind off the water is real
  • Research afterparties‚Äîofficial and unofficial events extend the weekend
  • GA+ and VIP available but GA experience is excellent

Getting There & Getting In

Pier 80 is in the Dogpatch/Bayview area, south of downtown SF. Rideshare is the easiest option. Official shuttles run from downtown locations.

  • Uber/Lyft: Expect surge pricing, especially at night‚Äîbudget $30-50 from downtown
  • Shuttles: Purchase in advance, they fill up
  • Parking: Limited on-site parking available for purchase
  • Entry: Wristband pickup available ahead of time to skip day-of lines

The Festival Grounds

Pier 80's industrial setting creates a unique atmosphere. Multiple stages including the main outdoor stage, the Warehouse (indoor techno), and additional stages featuring everything from experimental to hip-hop crossover.

"The Warehouse stage is basically a Berlin club dropped onto a San Francisco pier. The sound in there is incredible." ‚Äî r/Techno

Food, Water & Survival

Bay Area food standards apply‚Äîexpect quality. Water stations throughout. The wind and fog are real‚Äîbring a jacket even if it's warm during the day.

  • Free water refill stations (bring a bottle)
  • Food vendors are curated‚Äîbetter than average festival food
  • Bars serve quality drinks, not just well liquor
  • Earplugs recommended‚Äîsound systems are LOUD

The Crowd & Community

Portola attracts music-first attendees. Less rave culture, more club culture. You'll find industry people, serious collectors, and folks who flew in specifically for the lineup. Very LGBTQ+-friendly, fashion-forward, and generally older than mainstream EDM festivals.

After the Festival

San Francisco's club scene extends the experience. Official afterparties at venues like Public Works and 1015 Folsom feature artists from the lineup. The advantage of a city festival‚Äîthe party doesn't have to end.
