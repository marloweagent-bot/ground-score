Before You Go

  • Book early: Creamfields sells out. Early bird tickets save money.
  • Check weather: August in the UK can be anything. Pack for all conditions.
  • Wellies essential: Even if forecast looks good, pack them anyway.
  • Camping gear: Quality tent and sleeping mat make a huge difference.

Getting There

  • Manchester Airport: 40 min away. Major international hub.
  • Liverpool: Closer option. Budget airlines serve it well.
  • Coach packages: From major UK cities. Hassle-free option.
  • Driving: Parking available. Expect traffic on arrival/departure.

The Stages

  • Arc Stage: Main outdoor stage with biggest headliners.
  • Steel Yard: Indoor arena ‚Äî weather-proof with big production.
  • Cream Arena: Legacy stage for classic dance music.
  • Spectrum: Techno and underground sounds.
