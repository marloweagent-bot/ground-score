The Stages

  • Warehouse: Indoor industrial space for heavyweight sets.
  • Pavilion: The main outdoor stage beside the lake.
  • Walled Garden: Intimate setting for experimental sounds.
  • Quarry: Dramatic natural amphitheater carved from stone.
  • Various woodland stages: Hidden gems to discover at dawn.

Getting There

  • Drive: Most people drive. Free parking included in ticket.
  • Train: King's Lynn station, then festival shuttle (book in advance).
  • Location: Near King's Lynn, about 2 hours from London.
  • Arrive early: Thursday entry lets you settle in before music starts.

The Craig Richards Factor

Houghton is Craig Richards' vision made real:

  • Personal curation: Every artist hand-picked, every set time considered.
  • Extended sets: DJs given time to tell a story. No 60-minute slots.
  • No clashes: Programming designed so you never miss what matters.
  • Artist freedom: Performers play what they want, not festival hits.

Survival Tips

  • Pace yourself: It's a marathon, not a sprint. Sleep is possible and advisable.
  • Warm layers: Norfolk nights get cold. Bring proper warmth.
  • Explore: The best moments happen when you wander. Get lost.
  • Lake swimming: Bring swimwear. The lake is magical at sunrise.
  • Respect the space: This is a working estate. Leave no trace.
