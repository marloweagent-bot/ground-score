  • Secret Dreams: Related event at Legend Valley carrying some of Resonance's DNA ‚Äî electronic-focused with similar community vibes.
  • Essential Productions: The team behind Resonance continues to throw events and influence Ohio's scene.
  • The Crossover Lives: The jam-bass community Resonance cultivated continues at other festivals and shows.

If you were lucky enough to attend Resonance, you understand why it still matters. If you missed it, check out Secret Dreams or similar crossover events that carry the torch.

What Made Resonance Special

The magic was in the crossover.

  • Genre bridge: Jam bands AND bass music on the same lineup, respected equally.
  • Discovery engine: People regularly left with 10+ new favorite artists.
  • Community: Small enough to feel intimate, big enough for real production.
  • Booking courage: Willing to take risks on underground acts alongside headliners.

Notable Acts Over the Years

A legendary roster of performers:

  • Electronic: Tipper (multiple years), Emancipator, Daily Bread, Tycho, Mindex, Desert Dwellers
  • Jam: Papadosio (residents), STS9, Goose (before they blew up), The Floozies, Keller Williams
  • Crossover: Lotus, Break Science, EOTO ‚Äî artists who spoke both languages
"Great mix of the weird electronic music I love and the jam bands my friends really love. Everyone finds something at Resonance." ‚Äî r/festivals community member

The Troubled End

Resonance's final years weren't smooth.

  • 2022 Cancellation: Permitting issues forced a last-minute cancellation, upsetting ticket holders.
  • Refund drama: Some attendees struggled to get refunds, leading to chargebacks.
  • Venue shuffle: Moving from Legend Valley to Nelson Ledges for the final edition.
  • Announced as final: The 2023 edition was explicitly billed as the last.

For Those Who Miss It

Where to find similar vibes:

  • Secret Dreams (Legend Valley): Electronic-focused successor with similar community.
  • Tipper events: Follow Dave Tipper's standalone events for that specific magic.
  • Papadosio events: The band's own events carry some Resonance DNA.
  • The crossover spirit: Look for festivals booking both jam and bass ‚Äî they exist, they're just rare.
