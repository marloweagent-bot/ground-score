Before You Go

LAN is a two-night indoor/outdoor festival. Dallas weather in December is mild but can vary.

  • Book near Deep Ellum: The nearby entertainment district has hotels, restaurants, and afterparty potential.
  • Layer options: December Dallas can be 40-60¬∞F. Indoor stages are warm, outdoor can be chilly.
  • VIP worth it?: Sky Lounge, express entry, and elevated viewing. Worth considering for NYE night.
  • Two-day recommended: Single days available, but the full experience spans both nights.

Getting There & Getting In

Dallas Market Hall is at 2200 N Stemmons Fwy, centrally located in Dallas.

  • Fly into DFW: Major hub with competitive pricing. 20-30 minutes to venue.
  • Drive: Easy for Texas residents. Houston, Austin, San Antonio all within driving distance.
  • Parking: On-site parking available. Lots and box office open at 5PM.
  • Entry: Standard security. Arrive early on NYE to avoid peak lines.

The Festival Grounds

Dallas Market Hall offers indoor and outdoor spaces with multiple stages.

  • Main Stage (Indoor): Headliners and prime production. Climate controlled.
  • Outdoor Stage: Different vibe, fresh air, often bass-heavy programming.
  • Support Stages: Smaller rooms with emerging artists and different genres.

Food, Water & Survival

Standard festival logistics with Texas hospitality.

  • Water: Refill stations available. Bring an empty bottle or CamelBak.
  • Food: Multiple vendors with typical festival options. Prices are $12-18.
  • Deep Ellum: Pre-festival dinner options within 10 minutes. Great Texas BBQ nearby.
  • Weather: December Dallas is mild. Check forecast and layer accordingly.

The Crowd & Community

Texas rave community is passionate and welcoming.

  • Local love: Strong DFW scene with returning fans each year.
  • Regional draw: Houston, Austin, San Antonio crews make the drive.
  • Age range: 18+ crowd, diverse across ages and backgrounds.
  • Southern hospitality: Generally friendly, helpful vibes throughout.

After the Festival

Dallas offers options for continuing the party.

  • Deep Ellum: The entertainment district has bars, clubs, and late-night food.
  • Lost & Found: Email lostandfound@lightsallnight.com for any items left behind.
  • Outlet: LAN partners with Outlet Mental Health for support resources.
  • Recovery: Texas brunch game is strong. Sleep in, then feast.
