Camping Options

The camping is central to the Mysteryland experience.

  • Regular Camping: Bring your tent, set up with friends. The classic experience.
  • Easy Tent: Pre-pitched tent waiting for you. Just bring sleeping bag.
  • Dreamville: Premium camping with better facilities and themed areas.
  • Friendship Camping: Guaranteed spots together for groups.

The Stages

Something for every electronic taste.

  • Main Stage: The big names, massive production, headline sets.
  • Q-Dance: Hardstyle heaven. If you know, you know.
  • Desperados: Deep house and disco vibes.
  • The Healing Garden: Downtempo, yoga, recovery zone.
  • Secret Stages: Hidden areas to discover throughout the weekend.

  • Earplugs (for sleeping, the bass carries)
  • Wet wipes and dry shampoo
  • Portable phone charger
