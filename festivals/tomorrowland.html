The Stages

Each stage is a destination.

  • Main Stage: The centerpiece. New theme each year built at impossible scale.
  • Freedom Stage: Second largest. Often hosts techno and harder styles.
  • Atmosphere: Trance home stage.
  • Core: Charlotte de Witte's techno sanctuary.
  • Rose Garden: Deep house and melodic sounds.
  • Library: Underground and experimental.

DreamVille Camping

A city within the festival.

  • Magnificent Greens: Basic camping. Bring your tent.
  • Easy Tents: Pre-pitched and ready.
  • Montagoe: Luxury glamping with private facilities.
  • The Gathering: Thursday pre-party for DreamVille guests.

Getting Tickets

The hardest part of Tomorrowland is getting in.

  • Pre-registration: Required before main sale. Opens December.
  • Main sale: January/February. Sells out in minutes.
  • Global Journey: Travel packages with guaranteed tickets. More expensive but more reliable.
  • Waiting list: Official resale platform. Check regularly.
