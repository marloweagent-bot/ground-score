Before You Go

Getting tickets is the first challenge. Tomorrowland sells out in minutes during the worldwide sale. Your options:

  • Worldwide Sale: Happens in late January. Register early, join the queue, pray.
  • Belgian Pre-Sale: Reserved for Belgian residents.
  • Global Journey: Travel packages with guaranteed tickets (flights + accommodation). Expensive but reliable.
  • Waiting List: Register and hope for cancellations.

Getting There & Getting In

Belgium is incredibly accessible from anywhere in Europe.

  • Fly into Brussels (BRU): Major hub with connections worldwide. Shuttles to festival.
  • Antwerp: Closer option, 30 min to Boom.
  • Train: Boom has a train station with festival shuttles.
  • Global Journey: Includes all transport ‚Äî airport pickup to festival entry.

The Festival Grounds

16+ stages spanning every electronic genre. The main stage is the centerpiece, rebuilt each year around the annual theme.

  • Main Stage: Where headliners play. Massive, theatrical, unmissable.
  • Freedom Stage: Second biggest. Great for avoiding main stage crowds.
  • CORE: Underground techno and house in an intimate setting.
  • Atmosphere: Trance paradise.
  • The Rave Cave: Hardstyle and harder styles.
"Tomorrowland is not just a festival ‚Äî it's a parallel universe. The production, the people, the energy. Nothing compares." ‚Äî FestPass Research, 2024

Food, Water & Survival

  • Cashless: Everything runs on the festival bracelet. Load money before you go.
  • Water: Free water refill points throughout. Bring an empty bottle.
  • Food: Quality is excellent for a festival. Belgian waffles, frites, international options.
  • Weather: July in Belgium is mild but pack layers and rain gear just in case.

DreamVille (Camping)

DreamVille is legendary and highly recommended. It's a festival within a festival.

  • Magnificent Greens: Standard camping with your own tent.
  • Easy Tents: Pre-pitched tents for convenience.
  • Cabana/Relax Rooms: Boutique camping with real beds.
  • The Gathering: Pre-party the night before the festival starts.

After the Festival

Post-Tomorrowland recovery is real. Budget a rest day before flying home.

  • Brussels: Explore the city, eat Belgian chocolate, recover.
  • Antwerp/Bruges: Beautiful Belgian cities worth visiting.
  • Extended trip: Many combine Tomorrowland with Amsterdam or Paris.
