The Concept

Audiotistic has its origins in late 90s Southern California as a premier event uniting electronic and hip-hop culture. After joining the Insomniac family, the festival moved to the Bay Area and continues to bridge both worlds with lineups spanning house, dubstep, trap, and hip-hop.
