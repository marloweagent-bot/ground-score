The Sustainability Mission

DGTL isn't greenwashing ‚Äî they're actually leading the industry.

  • Circular economy: Aim to be fully circular by 2025. Waste becomes resource.
  • Plant-based focus: Meat-free food vendors reduce carbon footprint significantly.
  • Resource Dashboard: Real-time tracking of water, energy, and waste during the event.
  • Revolution Foundation: Educational platform sharing learnings with other festivals.

Getting There

NDSM is an experience before you even arrive.

  • Ferry: Free ferry from Amsterdam Centraal ‚Äî the 15-minute ride builds anticipation.
  • Bike: Dedicated parking. Amsterdam's favorite transport method.
  • Shuttle bus: From various city locations.
  • Public transport: Bus connections from Noord.
