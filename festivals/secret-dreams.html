Before You Go

Secret Dreams rewards the prepared ‚Äî know what you're getting into.

  • Know the sound: This isn't mainstream EDM. Research artists like Tipper, Of The Trees, and the jam-electronic crossover. Come for sonic exploration.
  • Book early: The fest has been growing and buzz is building. Lineup drops generate fast sales.
  • Weather prep: July in Ohio = hot and humid. Potentially muddy. Pack accordingly.
  • Art focus: Come ready to engage with visual art and installations, not just music.

Getting There & Getting In

Legend Valley is ~45 minutes east of Columbus in Thornville, OH.

  • Fly into Columbus (CMH): Most accessible airport. Rent a car ‚Äî you'll need it.
  • Drive from Midwest cities: Cleveland, Pittsburgh, Cincinnati all within 2-3 hours.
  • Camping: GA camping is across the road from the venue. Preferred camping is closer.
  • Entry: Standard festival security. Arrive early to beat crowds.

The Festival Grounds

Legend Valley is well-established ‚Äî decades of festival history, recently upgraded infrastructure.

  • Main Stage: Big sound, major headliners. Where Tipper, Pretty Lights perform.
  • Portal Stage: The aesthetic highlight ‚Äî intimate, immersive, bass-focused.
  • Art spaces: Interactive installations scattered throughout. Explore after dark.
  • Camping to venue: Walk across the road. Not bad, but prepare for the trek.

Food, Water & Survival

Standard festival logistics ‚Äî plan ahead.

  • Hydrate: Ohio humidity is real. Drink water constantly.
  • Food vendors: Adequate selection. Don't expect culinary highlights.
  • Bring supplies: Car camping means coolers work. Stock up.
  • Hearing protection: Sound systems are LOUD. Quality earplugs essential.

The Crowd & Community

Self-selecting for music nerds and art appreciators.

  • Knowledgeable fans: People here KNOW the artists. Expect deep-cut conversations.
  • Bass + jam fusion: Rare crossover crowd that appreciates both scenes.
  • Growing pains: As a younger fest, some organizational issues still being worked out.
  • Welcoming: Open, inclusive vibe. Good first-fest for the bass-curious.

After the Festival

Columbus offers recovery options.

  • Columbus: Decent food scene, breweries, hotels for recovery nights.
  • Decompression: This music hits different. Give yourself processing time.
  • Community: r/SecretDreams stays active year-round. Stay connected.
