Before You Go

Sunwaves is for the dedicated. Come prepared for a marathon.

  • Editions: Spring (May) and Summer (August) editions. Spring is considered the classic.
  • Tickets: Very affordable but sell out. Book day passes or full festival.
  • Accommodation: Hotels in Mamaia resort. Book ahead during festival dates.
  • Prepare: This is an endurance event. Rest before, pace during.

Getting There & Getting In

Not the most accessible festival, but worth the journey.

  • Flights: Constanta airport (limited) or Bucharest (300km drive).
  • Transfer: Bus or taxi from Bucharest to Mamaia (3+ hours).
  • Venue: Aza Beach in Mamaia resort area.

The Festival Grounds

Stripped back to essentials ‚Äî beach, tent, incredible sound.

  • Main stage: Covered tent with Funktion-One system and the world's best minimal DJs.
  • Beach: The Black Sea is right there for breaks and swimming.
  • Hours: Music runs essentially 24/7 for 5-6 days.

Food, Water & Survival

Marathon events require marathon preparation.

  • Food: Basic options on-site. Mamaia town has restaurants.
  • Hydration: Essential. Bring water to the beach.
  • Sleep: Return to hotel, rest, come back. Repeat for 5 days.
  • Substances: The crowd is experienced. Know your limits.

The Crowd & Community

The inner circle of underground techno.

  • Demographics: International techno pilgrims. Many returning veterans.
  • Knowledge: People know the DJs, know the sound, know what they came for.
  • Energy: Focused, hypnotic, communal. No posers survive 5 days.

After the Festival

Recovery is mandatory after a Sunwaves marathon.

  • Decompress: Stay an extra day in Mamaia. Sleep.
  • Beach: The Black Sea is beautiful for recovery swimming.
  • Memories: You'll process the experience for weeks after.
