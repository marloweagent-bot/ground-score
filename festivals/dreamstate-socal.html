Before You Go

Dreamstate requires less preparation than most festivals, but understanding the trance landscape helps.

  • Know your subgenres: Uplifting, tech-trance, psytrance, progressive ‚Äî each has a different energy. Research the lineup and identify your must-sees.
  • Check the afterparties: Dreamstate afterparties extend into the early morning. Academy LA and Exchange LA typically host official events.
  • Hotel strategy: Long Beach hotels fill up but aren't impossible. Downtown Long Beach or nearby Shoreline Village area keeps you walkable.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be dancing for hours. This is a marathon of movement, not standing and watching.

Getting There & Getting In

The Queen Mary Waterfront is one of the most accessible festival venues in SoCal.

  • Flying in: LAX is 25-30 minutes away. Long Beach Airport (LGB) is even closer for some routes.
  • Parking: On-site parking available but fills up. Arrive early or use rideshare.
  • Rideshare: Uber/Lyft work great here. Designated pickup/dropoff areas. Surge pricing is moderate compared to larger festivals.
  • Entry: Standard Insomniac security ‚Äî bag checks, metal detectors. Usually flows smoothly; arrive 30+ min before must-see sets.

The Festival Grounds

Compact, walkable, and designed for music discovery. You can genuinely explore every stage.

  • The Dream: Main stage. This is where the headliners play ‚Äî expect the biggest production and crowds.
  • The Vision: Focused on classic and uplifting trance. Often features legendary artists and emotional moments.
  • The Sequence: Psytrance central. Faster BPMs, more intense energy, dedicated psy community.
  • The Timeless: Rotating stage for special sets, classics, and unique programming.

Food, Water & Survival

November weather makes Dreamstate comfortable, but you're still dancing for hours.

  • Weather: Expect 55-65¬∞F at night. Bring a light jacket or hoodie ‚Äî the waterfront gets breezy.
  • Hydration: Water stations available. You'll sweat less than summer festivals but still need to hydrate.
  • Food: Standard festival vendors. Eat dinner before arriving to maximize dance time.
  • Ear protection: Sound systems are powerful. Bring quality earplugs ‚Äî you'll thank yourself.

The Crowd & Community

Dreamstate attracts a different crowd than mainstream EDM festivals.

  • Age range: Skews older ‚Äî expect many 25-45 year olds who've been in the scene for years.
  • Knowledge level: People know the music. They'll recognize deep cuts and cheer for specific melodies.
  • Dance floor energy: Eyes closed, hands up, full commitment. Less phone filming, more presence.
  • International travelers: Trance fans fly in from around the world. You'll meet people from Europe, Asia, South America.

After the Festival

The night doesn't end when Dreamstate closes.

  • Afterparties: Official afterparties at LA clubs run until 4-6AM. Check announcements closer to the event.
  • Recovery: Long Beach has great breakfast spots. Pike outlets and waterfront are walkable for daytime exploration.
  • Monday flights: If possible, book Monday afternoon departures. You'll want recovery time.
