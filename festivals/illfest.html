ILLfest is a city festival‚Äîno camping involved. That means you get real beds, real showers, and Austin's legendary food scene.

  • Check the art lineup: Specific muralists are announced‚Äîplan which ones to watch create
  • Hydrate: Late May in Texas is HOT. Start hydrating days before
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be on your feet all day on concrete
