Before You Go

  • DnB knowledge: You'll enjoy it more if you know the artists. Deep lineup rewards research.
  • Camping gear: Full festival camping. Bring proper gear or upgrade to glamping.
  • Prague connection: Consider days in Prague before/after. Great city, active DnB scene.
  • Earplugs: Essential. DnB is loud. Protect your hearing for 3 days of bass.

The New Venue (2025)

Lake Most replaces the Milovice Airfield, bringing natural beauty to the bass.

  • Lake access: Swimming available. Cool down between sessions.
  • Natural setting: Trees, water, grass ‚Äî more pleasant than airfield concrete.
  • Better camping: Improved facilities and layout.
  • Same massive production: Stages and sound unchanged.

Survival Tips

  • Pace yourself: 72 hours of 174 BPM is a marathon. Sleep some.
  • Explore stages: Main stage is obvious but smaller stages have special moments.
  • Czech beer: Cheap and excellent. Pilsner Urquell territory.
  • Community: DnB crowds are friendly. Make friends. Trade kandi if that's your thing.
