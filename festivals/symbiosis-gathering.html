Current Status

Symbiosis has been on hiatus since the Oregon Eclipse gathering in 2017. That event, aligned with the total solar eclipse, was intended as a landmark moment. Since then, there have been no confirmed future dates, though the community remains connected and hopeful.

The producers have always operated on an irregular schedule, appearing when conditions are right rather than annual obligation. This makes predicting a return difficult, but the legacy remains influential in the festival world.

The History

Symbiosis grew from 800 people at Cutter Scout Camp in 2005 to over 15,000 at the 10-year anniversary in 2015:

  • 2005: First gathering near Santa Cruz, 800 attendees
  • 2006-2007: Angels Camp, California
  • 2009: Camp Mather near Yosemite
  • 2012: Pyramid Lake, Nevada ‚Äî during an annular solar eclipse
  • 2013-2016: Woodward Reservoir, Oakdale CA ‚Äî the defining era
  • 2017: Oregon Eclipse ‚Äî collaboration with 10 international festivals

What Made It Special

Symbiosis pioneered several innovations that influenced transformational festivals globally:

  • Swimbiosis: Sound system pointed at the water, dancefloor extending to swimmers on rafts and boats
  • Art Boats: DIY floating vessels with sound systems, likely influenced by Burning Man's art cars
  • No Screens: Deliberate rejection of LED walls and projections ‚Äî only lighting and structure
  • International Collaborations: Formal partnerships with Rainbow Serpent, Boom, Universo Paralello
  • Zero Corporate Sponsors: Never compromised on this principle
"Symbiosis is a collaborative experiment ‚Äî an international gathering where the true value is people coming together. We call it a Gathering, not a festival, with intention." ‚Äî Bosque Hrbek, Co-founder

The Venue (Woodward Reservoir)

The 2,900-acre Woodward Reservoir became Symbiosis's defining home from 2013-2016:

  • Five-nubbed peninsula: Natural zoning created distinct areas
  • Open swimming: The reservoir was accessible throughout the event
  • September weather: California warmth without extreme heat
  • Natural ambiance: Water, sky, and land rather than urban infrastructure

Oregon Eclipse 2017

The most ambitious Symbiosis event brought together 10 international festival producers for a gathering aligned with the total solar eclipse:

  • Partners: Bass Coast, Beloved, Envision, Hadra, Noisily, Ometeotl, Origin, Rainbow Serpent, Re:Birth, Universo Paralello
  • Scale: 30,000+ attendees, 7 music stages, 40+ workshop areas
  • Innovation: Bitcoin ticket purchasing option
  • Legacy: Called "the most transcendental event in the known universe"

The Community

Symbiosis attracted a global tribe of festival veterans, permaculture practitioners, visionary artists, and conscious community builders:

  • Participatory culture: Bring your own art, join workshops, contribute
  • Family-friendly: Kids village with dedicated programming
  • Ecological ethos: Leave places better than you found them
  • International network: Connected to festivals worldwide
