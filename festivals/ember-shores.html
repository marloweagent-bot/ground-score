Before You Go

This is a destination event ‚Äî preparation is different from traditional festivals.

  • Passport required: Mexico means you need a valid passport. Apply early if you don't have one.
  • Split a room: 4-person rooms bring per-person cost down significantly (~$1,500 vs $3,000+).
  • Book early: Sells out quickly. Payment plans available.
  • Pack for resort + festival: Swimsuits, nice outfits for dinner, festival gear.

Getting There & Getting In

Fly into Cancun, shuttle to paradise.

  • Flights: CUN (Cancun) is well-served from most US cities. Book early for December rates.
  • Airport transfer: ~45 minutes to Riviera Maya. Shuttles and taxis available.
  • Check-in: Standard resort check-in. Wristbands distributed on site.
  • Resort access: Once checked in, everything is walkable. No transportation needed.

The Festival Experience

Music runs noon to midnight across beach, pool, and indoor stages.

  • Beach Stage: Main stage, ocean backdrop, sunset sets. The crown jewel.
  • Pool Stage: Daytime vibes, swim up to the music.
  • Indoor After-Parties: Late-night continuation in climate-controlled spaces.
  • No conflicts: One artist at a time. You see everyone ‚Äî or no one if you need pool time.

Food, Drinks & All-Inclusive Life

This is where destination festivals shine.

  • All included: Multiple restaurants, buffets, bars, room service ‚Äî all in your package.
  • Quality: 5-star resort food. Not festival food truck levels.
  • Unlimited drinks: Tropical cocktails flow freely. Pace yourself.
  • Real meals: Eat actual dinner between sets. Revolutionary.

The Crowd & Community

Self-selected for ILLENIUM superfans.

  • Illenial family: Everyone knows the lyrics. Everyone has a story about what the music means.
  • Emotional: Be prepared for collective catharsis. Crying is normal.
  • Welcoming: Solo travelers report easy connections. The community is tight.
  • Older crowd: 21+ requirement plus cost means fewer teenagers.

After the Festival

Extend your vacation or fly home relaxed.

  • Extra nights: Available at additional cost. Many people extend.
  • Recovery day: Build in time before your flight. Don't rush.
  • Excursions: Cenotes, ruins, snorkeling ‚Äî Mexico has options if you stay longer.
  • Post-event depression: Coming home to winter after paradise is rough. Prepare mentally.
