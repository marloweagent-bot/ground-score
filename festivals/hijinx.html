Before You Go

Winter festival logistics differ from summer.

  • Coat check: You'll need to check your coat. Plan for lines and potential waits.
  • Layers: It's December in Philly. Dress warm for outside, light for inside.
  • Book hotels downtown: Walking distance to the convention center is ideal.
  • Plan food: Philly has incredible food. Research cheesesteak spots.

The Convention Center

Indoor winter raving has trade-offs.

  • Multiple stages: Main stage, bass stage, house stage ‚Äî all under one roof.
  • Sound bleed: Expect some overlap between areas. Position yourself accordingly.
  • Climate controlled: No weather concerns. Consistent comfortable temps.
  • Capacity: Can feel crowded at peak times. Find your spot early for headliners.
