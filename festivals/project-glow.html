The Partnership

Project Glow is a collaboration between Insomniac (the EDC/Beyond Wonderland powerhouse) and Club Glow, the East Coast's dominant electronic music promoter. Club Glow has been cultivating DC's dance music scene for 20+ years through their flagship venue Echostage. Together, they launched Project Glow in Spring 2022.

The partnership brings Insomniac's production expertise and booking power to Club Glow's local infrastructure and community knowledge. It's a model that works ‚Äî and shows what regional promoters can achieve with the right backing.

The Three Stages

  • Eternal Stage: Main stage for headliners. New improved design in 2024 with pyro and fireworks. Gets crowded for big names.
  • Pulse Stage: Mid-size stage with diverse bookings. 2024's new leaf-adorned design. Good alternative when main stage is packed.
  • Secret Garden: Intimate house and techno haven. Where the real heads gather. Often the best vibes of the festival.
"The Secret Garden is where you find the community. Main stage is great, but the real connections happen in the intimate spaces." ‚Äî Project Glow regular

The Echostage Connection

The festival doesn't end at 11PM. Echostage hosts official afterparties that often rival the festival itself:

  • Extended sets: Headliners do 2+ hour sets in club environment
  • Intimate b2bs: 2024's Zedd b2b Knock2 was legendary
  • Production: Echostage recently upgraded with insane laser systems
  • Energy: Often more packed and hyped than the festival

Budget for afterparty tickets. This is half the experience.

Logistics & Tips

  • Getting there: Rideshare to RFK Festival Grounds. Expect a walk from drop-off.
  • Weather prep: April DC weather is unpredictable. Bring rain gear and layers.
  • VIP consideration: 2024 VIP had issues (sound bleed, limited food). GA+ might be better value.
  • Hydration: Water stations exist but can have lines. Bring empty CamelBak.
  • Pacing: Hot sun on Sunday, rain possible any day. Stay hydrated, rest when needed.

Known Issues (And Improvements)

Project Glow has been transparent about growth areas:

  • Sound bleed: 2024's new layout made VIP viewing problematic. Pulse/Eternal too close.
  • VIP experience: Limited food, no water stations, long restroom waits reported
  • Shade: Limited shade structures on sunny days
  • Walk distance: Getting longer from drop-off to gates each year

The festival listens to feedback ‚Äî each year has brought improvements. Expect continued iteration.

The DC Weekend

Make a trip of it. DC offers plenty beyond the festival:

  • Pre-game: Explore museums (free!), monuments, Georgetown
  • Afterparties: Echostage is the anchor, but Flash DC and Soundcheck also host events
  • Recovery: Brunch culture is strong in DC. Build in a rest day.
