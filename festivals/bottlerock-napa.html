Before You Go

BottleRock requires some planning, especially for accommodations.

  • Book hotels NOW: Napa hotels sell out months ahead and prices spike. Consider Sonoma, Vallejo, or even SF.
  • Set food/wine priorities: Download the app and plan your culinary experiences. Popular wine cabanas require timing.
  • 10PM curfew: Music ends at 10PM sharp. Plan afterparties separately ‚Äî BottleRock AfterDark shows happen in downtown Napa.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be standing and walking on grass all day. Skip the heels.

Getting There & Getting In

Napa isn't the most accessible, but it's manageable.

  • From SF: 1-1.5 hours depending on traffic. Shuttles available from the city.
  • Parking: On-site parking available but expensive. Off-site lots with shuttles are cheaper.
  • Rideshare: Works but surge pricing hits hard. Coordinate with groups.
  • VIP/Platinum: Get dedicated parking and golf cart transport ‚Äî genuinely worth it at this festival.

The Festival Grounds

The Napa Valley Expo is compact and well-organized.

  • Main Stage: Headliners play here. Gets crowded but good sightlines throughout.
  • JaM Cellars Stage: Second stage with strong mid-tier bookings.
  • Verizon Stage: More emerging artists.
  • Culinary Stage: The unique star ‚Äî celebrity chefs cook while artists perform. Don't miss it.
  • Wine Cabanas: 40+ local wineries set up shop. This is why you're really here.

Food, Water & Survival

This is where BottleRock dominates every other festival.

  • Food: Napa's best restaurants set up on-site. Quality is genuinely exceptional. Prices match ($20-40 entrees).
  • Wine: Master sommelier curated selections. Rare bottles available. This is Napa ‚Äî the wine is serious.
  • Hydration: Water stations available. The wine makes it easy to forget to hydrate ‚Äî don't.
  • Pro tip: Pace yourself. It's easy to get wine-drunk by 3PM and miss headliners.

The Crowd & Community

A distinctly different festival demographic.

  • Age range: Skews 30-50. This is a grown-up festival.
  • Energy: Wine-buzz chill, not EDM intensity. Sing-alongs, not moshing.
  • Dress code: Wine country casual. Sundresses and button-downs, not rave gear.
  • Vibe: Friendly, relaxed, slightly bougie. Everyone's here for a good time, not to rage.

After the Festival

The 10PM curfew means the night doesn't end at the venue.

  • BottleRock AfterDark: Official afterparties at downtown Napa venues. Smaller acts, late-night dancing.
  • Downtown Napa: Restaurants and bars stay open. Good options within walking distance.
  • Wine country recovery: Book a spa day for Monday. You'll need it.
