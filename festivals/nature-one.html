The Stages

20+ areas covering the full electronic spectrum.

  • Open Air Floor: 30,000+ capacity main stage. The iconic Nature One moment.
  • Century Hall: Massive indoor hangar. Hard-hitting techno and trance.
  • Bunker stages: Various floors in former military structures. Dark, intense vibes.
  • House Floor: Dedicated to house music in all forms.
  • Hardstyle Floor: For the harder styles. Bring ear protection.

Camping

Camping is essential to the Nature One experience.

  • General camping: Massive fields. Arrive early for prime spots near stages.
  • Quiet camping: For those who actually want to sleep.
  • VIP camping: Better facilities, closer to action.
  • Car camping: Park next to your tent in designated areas.

Getting There

  • From Frankfurt: 1.5 hours by car. Shuttles available.
  • From Cologne: 2 hours drive.
  • Train: To Koblenz, then shuttle to site.
  • Airport: Frankfurt-Hahn (FHN) is closest, Frankfurt (FRA) has more flights.

Pro Tips

  • Pace yourself: 40 hours is real. Sleep is necessary.
  • Download the app: With 20+ stages, you need the schedule.
  • Bring layers: Hot days, cool forest nights.
  • Cash backup: Card systems can fail at peak times.
  • Explore bunkers: The hidden stages have the best discoveries.
