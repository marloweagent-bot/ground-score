Before You Go

Beloved asks you to arrive with intention. This isn't background entertainment ‚Äî it's a container for transformation.

  • Set intentions: What are you seeking? Healing, connection, spiritual growth? Know before you go.
  • Review programming: Workshops fill up. Know what you want to attend.
  • Physical prep: If you plan to do yoga daily, start stretching now.
  • Open heart: Leave cynicism at home. Sincerity is the currency here.

Getting There

Tidewater is in the Oregon Coast Range, about 2.5 hours from Portland.

  • From Portland: Drive southwest through wine country. Beautiful route.
  • Fly in: Portland (PDX) is the closest major airport.
  • Carpools: The community often organizes rideshares. Check forums.
  • Remote is the point: The journey is part of the transition into sacred space.

The Festival Grounds

Private forestland creates an ephemeral village in the Coast Range:

  • Main Temple: Central gathering space for music and community
  • Workshop Areas: Intimate spaces for healing work and education
  • Yoga Space: Daily practice in nature
  • Camping: Throughout the forest, find your spot under the trees
