Getting There

  • Tube: Mile End (Central, District, Hammersmith & City) or Bethnal Green (Central).
  • Overground: Hackney Wick or Cambridge Heath.
  • Bus: Routes 8, 277, 425, D6, S2 serve the area.
  • Cycling: Excellent bike infrastructure. Secure parking available.

In The Neighbourhood

APE runs free midweek events between music days:

  • Free entry: No ticket needed for "In the Neighbourhood" sessions.
  • Activities: Food markets, sports, fitness, art, comedy, and local vendors.
  • Family-friendly: Kids' activities during daytime hours.
  • Local focus: Showcases East London's creative community.

Festival Areas

  • East Stage: Main stage for headliners. Massive production and sound.
  • West Stage: Second stage for support acts and genre showcases.
  • Tent Stages: Intimate settings for electronic acts and DJs.
  • Comedy Tent: Stand-up and entertainment throughout the day.
  • Food Village: Curated selection of London's best street food.

Survival Tips

  • Pick your days: Each day has a distinct identity. Research lineups carefully.
  • Early for main stage: Prime spots for headliners fill up early.
  • Explore tent stages: Often where you'll discover new favorites.
  • Local afterparties: Hackney venues often host official continuations.
  • Layers: August evenings can cool down. Pack accordingly.
