The Stages

Eight unique areas covering the electronic spectrum.

  • Mainstage: The headliner stage with maximum production. Think Tomorrowland-level spectacle.
  • Resistance: Techno and underground sounds. For the serious heads.
  • Olympus (new 2025): Themed immersive experience with Greek mythology design.
  • Hardcore: Hardstyle, hardcore, gabber. The hard dance destination.
  • Trance: For the uplifting trance community.

Beat the Heat

August in Valencia is seriously hot. Plan accordingly.

  • Hydrate constantly: Bring reusable bottles. Refill stations available.
  • Arrive evening: The festival really starts at sunset. Beach during the day.
  • Shade tent: If camping, invest in a quality shade structure.
  • Light clothing: Breathable fabrics essential. Nights cool down.

Camping Options

Multiple camping tiers available.

  • Standard camping: Bring your own tent. Basic facilities.
  • Premium camping: Better location, more space, upgraded facilities.
  • Glamping: Pre-pitched tents with beds. Festival luxury.
  • Cullera town: Hotels and apartments available but book early.

Getting There

Valencia is your gateway.

  • Fly: Valencia Airport (VLC) is the closest. Budget carriers serve it well.
  • Shuttle: Official shuttles from Valencia city to Cullera.
  • Car: Parking available but expect traffic on peak entry days.
  • Train: Regional trains to Cullera from Valencia.
