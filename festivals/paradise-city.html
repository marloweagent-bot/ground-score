The Sustainability Mission

Paradise City leads by example.

  • B-Corp certified: One of few festivals with this certification.
  • Circular economy: Waste as resource, not problem.
  • Local sourcing: Food, materials, services from Belgium.
  • Carbon negative: Working toward measurable impact reduction.

The Setting

Ribaucourt Castle deserves its own section.

  • Castle grounds: Formal gardens become dance floors.
  • Woodland stages: Hidden among the trees.
  • Meadows: Open air dancing under Belgian skies.
  • The castle itself: Architectural backdrop for everything.
