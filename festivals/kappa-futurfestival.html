The Venue: Parco Dora

A former Fiat Ferriere steel plant transformed into one of Europe's most unique festival venues.

  • Industrial relics: Massive cooling towers, steel structures remain as art.
  • Green integration: Nature has reclaimed parts of the industrial site.
  • Multiple areas: Different sections offer distinct environments.
  • Urban accessibility: 15 minutes from Turin city center.

Turin as Festival City

  • Historic center: Baroque architecture, Egyptian museum, excellent food.
  • Aperitivo culture: Pre-festival drinks with Italian style.
  • Affordable: More budget-friendly than Milan or Rome.
  • Afterparties: Turin's clubs extend the experience into the night.

Getting There

  • Turin Airport (TRN): Budget carriers, 30 min to city.
  • Milan Malpensa (MXP): More flights, 90 min by bus/train.
  • Train: High-speed trains from Milan, Rome, other Italian cities.
  • Public transport: Metro and buses reach Parco Dora area.
