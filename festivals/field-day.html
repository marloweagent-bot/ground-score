Getting There

  • Tube: Brixton (Victoria Line) then 15-minute walk or bus.
  • Overground: Herne Hill is closest, direct access to park.
  • Bus: Routes 37, 68, 196, 322, 468 serve the area.
  • Cycling: Bike parking available. Excellent cycling neighborhood.

The 2025 Move

Field Day 2025 relocates to Brockwell Park after years at Victoria Park:

  • New location: South London's Brockwell Park, a Grade II listed heritage space.
  • Bank Holiday Weekend: Part of the Brockwell Live series.
  • Different vibe: Expect the intimacy to remain but with a fresh setting.
  • Local area: Brixton and Herne Hill offer great pre/post options.

What to Expect

  • Multiple stages: Outdoor and tented, each with distinct programming.
  • Dancing focus: Good space to move. People actually dance.
  • Music-first: Minimal brand activations and distractions.
  • Intimate scale: Never feels crushingly overcrowded.

Survival Tips

  • Arrive early: The undercard is part of the experience. Don't just come for headliners.
  • Explore all stages: The smallest tent might have the best set.
  • Weather prepared: May can be unpredictable. Layers and waterproof advisable.
  • Afterparties: South London clubs often host official continuations.
