The Story Behind the Name

On March 13, 1997, thousands of Phoenix residents witnessed a series of lights in the sky that remain unexplained to this day. The "Phoenix Lights" became one of the most famous UFO sightings in American history. The festival embraces this legacy with cosmic theming, alien imagery, and otherworldly vibes.

It's more than just branding ‚Äî the theme gives the festival a unique identity and encourages creative expression. When else can you dress like an extraterrestrial and fit right in?

Relentless Beats

Phoenix Lights is produced by Relentless Beats, Arizona's leading electronic music promoter. RB brings major acts to Phoenix year-round and produces several festivals including Decadence Arizona (NYE), Goldrush (Labor Day), and BOO! Arizona (Halloween).

  • Track record: 15+ years promoting in Arizona
  • Booking power: Brings top-tier headliners to the Southwest
  • Community focus: Built strong local scene relationships
"RB has been Arizona's dance music backbone for years. They know the local crowd and book accordingly." ‚Äî Arizona rave community

The Venue: Rawhide Event Center

Located in Chandler (about 30 minutes southeast of downtown Phoenix), Rawhide is a Western-themed entertainment complex that transforms for the festival:

  • Pros: Dedicated event space, parking available, away from residential areas
  • Cons: Some feel the venue is cramped compared to the space available at the nearby raceway
  • Layout: Stages positioned in the parking/event area outside the main complex
  • Facilities: Standard festival infrastructure with room for improvement

What to Wear

The cosmic theme means creative freedom:

  • Space/Alien costumes: Go as wild as you want ‚Äî body paint, prosthetics, full costumes
  • Holographic/Iridescent: Anything that catches light
  • LED accessories: Light-up everything fits the vibe
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be dancing on asphalt/grass ‚Äî plan accordingly
  • Layers: Desert temperatures drop at night

Logistics Tips

  • Hydration: April desert heat is manageable but drink water constantly
  • Clear bags: Security enforces clear bag policy strictly
  • Rideshare: Surge pricing hits hard at closing time ‚Äî consider coordinating rides
  • Hotels: Book in Chandler or Tempe for proximity; downtown Phoenix is farther
  • Pregame: Arizona has solid bars and restaurants for pre-festival hangs

Honest Assessment

Phoenix Lights is a solid regional festival, but some attendees note it hasn't reached its potential:

  • Production: Good but not great ‚Äî fans of Insomniac events may find it underwhelming
  • Venue: Functional but could be more spacious
  • Price-to-experience: Fair value, but some want more for their money
  • Local support: Strong Arizona scene shows up regardless

If you go in with regional festival expectations (not EDC expectations), you'll have a great time. It's Arizona's event, and the community makes it special.
