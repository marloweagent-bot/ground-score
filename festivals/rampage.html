Two Events

Rampage runs twice yearly.

  • Rampage Weekend (Feb): Indoor at Sportpaleis. Arena experience. Two nights of bass.
  • Rampage Open Air (July): Outdoor festival format. Three days, camping available.
  • Same quality: Both editions deliver the biggest names in bass music.

Survival Tips

  • Earplugs: ESSENTIAL. The bass is loud. Protect your hearing.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be on your feet for hours.
  • Hydration: The venue gets hot. Water is your friend.
  • Pace yourself: The energy is intense. Take breaks if needed.
