Before You Go

San Diego makes festival prep easy.

  • Book early for summer: San Diego hotels fill up. Plan ahead.
  • Sunscreen required: San Diego sun is intense. Reapply throughout the day.
  • Light layers: Warm days, cool ocean breeze at night.
  • Comfortable shoes: You'll be dancing on grass and concrete.

Getting There & Getting In

San Diego is absurdly accessible.

  • San Diego International (SAN): 10 minutes from downtown. One of America's most convenient airports.
  • Rideshare: Works perfectly. Short rides, manageable surge.
  • Trolley: San Diego's light rail gets you close for cheap.
  • Walking: If staying downtown, you might be able to walk.

The Festival Grounds

Waterfront Park or Petco Park ‚Äî both deliver San Diego vibes.

  • Waterfront Park: The CRSSD venue. Green grass, bay views, fountain wading between sets.
  • Petco Park: Baseball stadium transformed. Different energy, same quality.
  • Two stages: North and South with distinct sounds. Easy to move between.
  • The fountains: Yes, you can wade in them. Yes, you should.

Food, Water & Survival

San Diego culture meets festival logistics.

  • Craft beer: This is San Diego ‚Äî the beer selection is excellent.
  • Food vendors: Quality variety. San Diego takes food seriously.
  • Water stations: Available throughout. The fountains also help with cooling.
  • Weather management: Sun is intense. Shade is limited. Plan accordingly.

The Crowd & Community

San Diego's house and techno community is real.

  • CRSSD veterans: Many attendees are CRSSD regulars. They know what they're getting.
  • San Diego locals: Strong local support. This is their party.
  • LA spillover: SoCal heads make the trip. Worth the drive.
  • Chill vibes: San Diego energy is inherently relaxed. Less aggro than LA.

After the Festival

San Diego doesn't stop when the music ends.

  • Gaslamp Quarter: Bars, restaurants, nightlife right next to the venue.
  • Craft breweries: San Diego is craft beer capital. Explore.
  • Beach recovery: Coronado, La Jolla, Pacific Beach. Sun, sand, sleep.
  • Tacos: San Diego tacos are elite. Find the good ones.
