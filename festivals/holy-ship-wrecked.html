üé´ Unique Booking Process

Holy Ship! Wrecked requires a room reservation, not a standard ticket. Packages include your room at Hard Rock Riviera Maya plus all-inclusive food, drinks, and festival access. Prices are per-person based on room occupancy. A $250 deposit secures your spot with full payment due later.

Before You Go

This is a destination festival in Mexico. Plan accordingly.

  • Passport required: Valid US passport needed for Mexico. Check expiration dates early.
  • Book together: Room packages are per-person. Coordinate with your crew for room assignments.
  • Payment plans: $250 deposit to hold, then payment plans available for the balance.
  • Room categories: Options range from standard to suites. Price varies accordingly ($1,700-3,700).
  • Pack light: It's beach and pool vibes. Swimsuits, resort casual, comfortable dancing shoes.

Getting There

Fly into Canc√∫n International Airport (CUN) ‚Äî free shuttles handle the rest.

  • Flights to CUN: Direct flights from most major US cities. Book early for best prices.
  • Airport shuttles: Free transfers included with your package. No Uber needed.
  • Arrival timing: Check official guidance for shuttle schedules and check-in windows.
  • Check-in: Standard resort check-in at Hard Rock. Room ready, wristband, you're set.

The Resort Experience

Hard Rock Riviera Maya becomes a 4-day festival grounds with all resort amenities active.

  • Beach stage: Main stage on the sand with Caribbean backdrop. Sunset sets are legendary.
  • Pool parties: Pool-side stages running during the day. Drinks in hand, music on point.
  • Indoor venues: Intimate after-hours and late-night sets throughout the resort.
  • Your room: A real hotel room, steps away. Nap when needed, shower whenever.
