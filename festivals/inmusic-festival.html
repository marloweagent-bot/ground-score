The Setting: Lake Jarun

INmusic takes place on an island in Lake Jarun, a recreational lake on Zagreb's outskirts. The city built this area for the 1987 Summer Universiade, and now it hosts one of Europe's best-value rock festivals. You can literally swim in the lake between sets.

The Music

INmusic punches well above its weight class in booking:

  • Recent headliners: The Cure, Foo Fighters, Arctic Monkeys, Nick Cave, The Killers
  • Undercard: Strong indie, electronic, and regional acts
  • Diverse genres: Rock-focused but not exclusively

Getting There

  • Zagreb Airport: 20 minutes, well-connected European hub
  • City center: Tram 17 to Jarun, 15 minutes
  • Festival shuttles: From airport and central locations

Camping & Accommodation

  • Festival camping: On the island, included with camping passes
  • Zagreb hotels: Easy tram ride to festival
  • Airbnb: Good options throughout Zagreb

The Zagreb Bonus

Unlike remote festivals, INmusic is at a capital city's doorstep. Zagreb is underrated ‚Äî Austro-Hungarian architecture, great food scene, excellent coffee culture, and genuinely affordable. Consider arriving early or staying late to explore.
