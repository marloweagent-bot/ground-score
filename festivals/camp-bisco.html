History

Camp Bisco was born from The Disco Biscuits' ambition to create their own festival ‚Äî a common jam band move that became something much larger.

  • 1999: First Camp Bisco at a campground in Pennsylvania
  • Early 2000s: Nomadic years at various Northeast venues
  • 2011-2014: Indian Lookout Country Club in Mariaville, NY
  • 2015-2019: Montage Mountain era in Scranton, PA ‚Äî the peak years
  • 2020: Announced and postponed due to COVID
  • 2021: Cancelled outright ‚Äî never returned in original form
  • 2023: BISCOLAND launches as spiritual successor

What Made It Special

Camp Bisco was the patient zero of jamtronica festivals ‚Äî watching electronic and jam scenes merge in real-time.

"Camp Bisco crowds like ketamine... a lot. Those folks were wild." ‚Äî Security officer quoted on r/festivals, 2024
  • Jamtronica pioneers: The Disco Biscuits literally invented the genre; the fest showcased it
  • Bass integration: Early adopter of Bassnectar, Tipper, and bass music before it was everywhere
  • Six Biscuits sets: No other festival gave you this much of your favorite band
  • The waterpark: Unique amenity that defined the Montage Mountain experience
  • Northeast institution: For 20 years, THE camping festival for the corridor

The Reputation

Camp Bisco was legendary ‚Äî and notorious. The crowd was committed in ways that made security nervous.

  • Wook central: If you know the term, Camp Bisco defined it
  • Party hard culture: This was not a wellness festival
  • Tight community: Veterans returned year after year
  • Not for everyone: First-timers were either converted or overwhelmed

The End & BISCOLAND

Camp Bisco 2020 was announced, then COVID cancelled it. It never returned to Montage Mountain.

  • 2020-2022: Pandemic limbo, no Camp Bisco
  • 2023: Disco Biscuits launch BISCOLAND at Wonderland Forest, NY
  • 2024: BISCOLAND expands to 3 days, builds new traditions
  • The spirit lives: Different venue, same band, similar crossover booking

Alternatives for Camp Bisco Fans

  • BISCOLAND: The official successor ‚Äî multiple Biscuits sets, Wonderland Forest NY
  • Secret Dreams: Similar bass/jam crossover at Legend Valley
  • Resonance: Jam-focused with electronic elements
  • Hulaween: Suwannee's jam/electronic hybrid
  • Peach Music Festival: Northeast jam focus (Scranton area)
