Before You Go

Electric Forest is a camping festival ‚Äî there's no hotel option. You're in for 4 days of outdoor living.

  • Camping gear: Tent, sleeping pad, sleeping bag rated for Michigan June nights (can get chilly). Or upgrade to Good Life camping for extra amenities.
  • Weather prep: Michigan weather is unpredictable. Bring rain gear, sun protection, and layers. Evacuations have happened.
  • Tickets sell out fast: Loyalty program gives returning attendees first access. General sale usually sells out within hours.

The Forest Experience

Sherwood Forest is the heart of Electric Forest ‚Äî a real forest transformed into an illuminated wonderland.

"I said 'Wow, look at that!' at least 3000 times during the week. The lit-up trees, the hammocks, the atmosphere ‚Äî it's unmatched in festivals."‚Äî Electric Forest attendee, 2024
  • Main stages: Ranch Arena (main), Sherwood Court (electronic), Tripolee (bass), Observatory (intimate)
  • Forest stages: Hidden stages that appear between trees. Some of the best sets happen here.
  • The Hangar: Indoor stage and art gallery ‚Äî perfect for weather breaks and intimate discoveries.
  • Extras: Dream Emporium, kayaking, yoga, scavenger hunts, workshops, and secret experiences you'll discover on your own.

The Community

Electric Forest isn't just a festival ‚Äî it's a community. The "Forest Family" concept is real.

  • Camp neighbors become friends: 4 days of camping creates bonds. Bring extra supplies to share.
  • Kandi and trading: PLUR culture is alive. Make kandi, bring gifts, participate in the giving.
  • Return rate is massive: Many attendees have been coming for 5-10+ years. You'll see familiar faces.

Survival Tips

  • Arrive early on Day 1: Line to get in can be brutal. Early arrival means better camping spots.
  • Bring a wagon: The walk from car to campsite can be far. Wagons are essential.
  • Pace yourself: It's 4 days. Don't peak on night one. The best sets are often late weekend.
  • Explore the forest at night: This is when the magic happens. Get lost intentionally.
