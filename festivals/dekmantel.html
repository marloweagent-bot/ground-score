The Stages

Each stage at Dekmantel is a world unto itself.

  • Main Stage: The flagship ‚Äî largest acts but still intimate by festival standards.
  • UFO: Iconic flying saucer structure. Deep house and leftfield. The most photographed stage.
  • Greenhouse: Glass structure with tropical plants. Ambient, experimental, downtempo.
  • Selectors: For the deep diggers. Rare groove, world music, boundary-pushing selections.
  • Boiler Room: Broadcast stage. Watch your favorite sets go viral in real-time.

Beyond the Weekend

Dekmantel is a five-day experience, not just a festival.

  • Wednesday-Thursday: Club nights throughout Amsterdam. Smaller, intimate shows.
  • Friday-Sunday: The main festival at Amsterdamse Bos.
  • Aan 't IJ: Sunday afterparty at the waterfront. Free entry, perfect wind-down.

Travel Tips

  • Get an OV-chipkaart: Dutch public transport card. Works on trams, buses, metro to the festival.
  • Bike rental: This is Amsterdam. Cycle to the Bos like a local.
  • Book early: Hotels fill months ahead. Consider Airbnb or hostels in Amsterdam.
  • Explore the city: Amsterdam's clubs, restaurants, and culture are part of the experience.

Packing Essentials

  • Rain jacket (Dutch summer weather)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Layers (forest gets cool at night)
  • Cash (some vendors are cash-only)
  • Earplugs (the sound systems are serious)
