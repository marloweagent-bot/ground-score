Before You Go

  • July heat: Italian summer is hot. Lightweight clothes, sunscreen, hydrate.
  • Book central Turin: Venue is in the city. Stay nearby, walk home.
  • Afterparties: Official events at Turin clubs. Book ahead.
  • Extend your trip: Turin deserves exploration. Museums, Fiat heritage, amazing food.

The Venue

Parco Dora was a Fiat factory until the 1990s. Now it's a public park preserving industrial heritage.

  • Main stage: Under preserved factory roof. Iconic steel columns frame the DJ.
  • Secondary stages: Spread through green areas and industrial structures.
  • Sunset: Watch the light change on rust and steel. Golden hour is magical.
"Dancing under rusted Fiat factory columns as the sun sets behind the Alps. KFF creates moments impossible anywhere else." ‚Äî MyFestMap Research, 2024

Turin Beyond the Festival

  • Food: Piedmont cuisine is exceptional. Agnolotti, vitello tonnato, gianduja.
  • Wine: Barolo and Barbaresco country is nearby. Wine tours available.
  • Culture: Egyptian Museum (second largest worldwide), National Cinema Museum.
  • Architecture: Baroque city center, Lingotto building, modern urban design.
