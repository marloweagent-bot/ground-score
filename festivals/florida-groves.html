Before You Go

Florida Groves is a hotel festival ‚Äî no camping, just two days of music with Orlando at your doorstep.

  • Tickets: GA starts around $150, VIP options available. Tiered pricing ‚Äî buy early for best deals.
  • Hotels: Tons of options near the fairgrounds and throughout Orlando. Airport hotels work great.
  • When to book: Orlando has hotel inventory, but weekends can fill up. Book at least a month out.
  • April weather: Mid-80s and sunny. Hydrate, wear sunscreen, bring sunglasses.

Getting There & Getting In

Orlando accessibility is a major plus.

  • Fly into: Orlando International (MCO) is a major hub with cheap flights from everywhere.
  • Getting around: Uber/Lyft work great. Rental cars are cheap if you want flexibility.
  • Parking: On-site parking available if driving.
  • Entry: Wristband pickup available early or at gates. Lines manageable for the size.

The Festival Grounds

Four stages offer distinct vibes across the Central Florida Fairgrounds.

  • Main Stage: The centerpiece with biggest production and headliners. Open space with good sightlines.
  • Pavilion Stage: Covered/shaded stage for house and melodic sets. Essential for escaping midday sun.
  • Bass Yard: Intimate space for dubstep, riddim, and heavy bass. The rail here goes hard.
  • Fourth Stage: Rotating concepts and local showcases. Worth exploring for discovery.

Food, Water & Survival

Standard festival operations ‚Äî plan accordingly.

  • Water: Free refill stations available. Bring a CamelBak or empty bottle.
  • Food: Vendor variety typical of fairground events. Budget $15-20 per meal.
  • Pro tip: Eat a real meal before entering. Hit up Orlando restaurants after.
  • Florida sun: April is hot. Sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, water ‚Äî the usual survival kit.

The Crowd & Community

Florida's local electronic scene turns out strong.

  • Local flavor: Big contingent from Florida bass and house communities. Tampa, Miami, Jacksonville all represented.
  • Vibe: Friendly and welcoming. The "still growing" energy means less ego than mega-festivals.
  • Age range: All ages event, but crowd skews 18-30.
  • What to wear: Festival casual. Costumes appreciated but not required.

After the Festival

You're in Orlando ‚Äî options are endless.

  • Afterparties: Check local venues and the festival's official after-events.
  • Recovery: Disney Springs, Universal CityWalk, or just sleep in your hotel.
