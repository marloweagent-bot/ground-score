Five days of underground electronic music on the Croatian coast.

The Stages

  • The Clearing: Main outdoor stage in a natural amphitheater. Sunrise sets here are legendary.
  • The Void: Indoor club space with powerful sound system. Headliners and extended sets.
  • The Garden: Open-air stage among olive trees. Perfect for afternoon-to-evening sessions.
  • Mungo's Arena: Dub and bass music focus. Sound system culture showcase.
  • Boat Parties: Daily sailings across the Adriatic. Book early, they sell out.

Camping & Accommodation

Multiple options for every budget:

  • On-site camping: Basic and premium plots available. Showers and facilities included.
  • Apartments in Tisno: The town has numerous rentals walking distance from venue.
  • Hotel packages: Available through festival for more comfort.
  • Pro tip: Book Tisno accommodation early ‚Äî it fills up months ahead.

Getting There

  • Fly to Split: Most common option, 1.5 hours drive. Major European connections.
  • Fly to Zadar: Slightly closer, good budget airline options.
  • Festival shuttles: Run from both airports. Book with ticket.
  • Rental car: Useful if exploring Croatia before/after.

What to Know

  • Book boat parties early: They sell out fast and are essential to the experience.
  • Pace yourself: Five days of late nights. The sea is your recovery tool.
  • Bring earplugs: Sound systems are serious. Protect your hearing for day five.
  • Cash backup: Some vendors are card-only, others cash. Bring both.
  • Explore Tisno: Great restaurants for proper meals outside festival food.
