The Stages

  • Orange Stage: The iconic main stage. One of Europe's biggest. Headliners only.
  • Arena: Major indoor venue for significant acts.
  • Apollo: Electronic and alternative focus.
  • Avalon: World music, folk, and roots.
  • Gloria: Hip-hop and urban sounds.
  • Rising: Emerging talent ‚Äî tomorrow's headliners today.

Getting There

  • Copenhagen: Fly into Copenhagen Airport (CPH), then 30 min train to Roskilde.
  • Direct trains: Regular service from Copenhagen Central.
  • Shuttle buses: Run between Roskilde station and festival site.
  • Driving: Possible but not recommended. Train is easier.

Camp City

Roskilde's camping is legendary:

  • Warm-up days: First four days are camping-only with emerging acts.
  • Camp areas: Each has its own character. Research beforehand.
  • Bring gear: Tent, sleeping bag, rain gear essential.
  • Dream City: Glamping option with pre-pitched tents.

Survival Tips

  • Arrive early: Warm-up days are essential for getting the full experience.
  • Rain gear: Danish weather. Be prepared for everything.
  • Pace yourself: Eight days is a marathon. Sleep when you can.
  • Volunteer: Consider volunteering for free ticket. Community experience.
  • Rising Stage: Check it daily. Discover before everyone else.
