What Made It Special

SnowGlobe was the only major EDM festival set in a winter wonderland. The combination of snow, mountains, NYE countdown, and world-class DJs created an irreplaceable experience.

  • Unique winter setting unlike any other US festival
  • NYE countdown at elevation with snow falling
  • Skiing/snowboarding access during the day
  • Lake Tahoe casino resorts as basecamp

The Cold Weather Challenge

SnowGlobe wasn't for everyone. Temperatures could drop below freezing, and proper gear was essential.

  • Multiple layers, ski gear, hand warmers required
  • Rotating into warming tents was strategy
  • Alcohol + cold = hypothermia risk
  • The shared struggle created community

Why It Ended

Noise complaints, permit issues, and logistical challenges with the city of South Lake Tahoe ultimately ended SnowGlobe. The festival failed to meet deadlines for addressing community concerns.
