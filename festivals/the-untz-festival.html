Before You Go

The Untz rewards those who do their homework.

  • Research the lineup: You may not know these names. That's the point. Listen before you go.
  • 3-day pass for Friday: Friday requires a 3-day pass. Plan accordingly.
  • Car camping optional: Walk-in camping is free with ticket. Premium car camping costs extra.
  • California May: Warm days, cool nights. Layers and sun protection both needed.

Getting There & Getting In

Northern California ‚Äî accessible but requires driving.

  • From Sacramento: ~60-90 minutes. Easy day-of drive.
  • From SF Bay Area: ~2-2.5 hours. Worth the trek.
  • Flying in: Sacramento (SMF) is closest. Rent a car.
  • Entry: Friday gates open at 1pm for 3-day. Saturday at 9am for 2-day.

The Festival Grounds

Sycamore Ranch (formerly at Mariposa) ‚Äî intimate and well-designed.

  • Main Stage: Where headliners and biggest names play.
  • Label Takeovers: Collective and label showcases on secondary stages.
  • Layout: Compact, everything walkable, never feels overcrowded.
  • Music hours: 8pm Friday to 4am Monday. Pace yourself.

Food, Water & Survival

Standard camping festival logistics.

  • Water: Bring your own supply and hydration pack. Stations available.
  • Food: Vendors present but bring camp food for best experience.
  • Camp setup: Premium car camping has electricity and water hookups.
  • Pack out: All vehicles must exit by 1pm Monday.

The Crowd & Community

The deep diggers of bass music.

  • Knowledgeable: People here know their stuff. Great conversations await.
  • Welcoming: Small size means inclusive community.
  • Artist accessible: You might camp next to someone playing that night.
  • No posers: Everyone found this place by caring about the music.

After the Festival

New favorite artists guaranteed.
