Getting There

SW4 couldn't be easier to reach:

  • Tube: Clapham Common (Northern Line) is literally at the gates.
  • Bus: Multiple routes serve Clapham. 137, 155, 355, G1.
  • Train: Clapham Junction is a short bus ride away.
  • Night Transport: Night tubes run until around 5am on weekends.

The Stages

  • Main Stage: The big names and headline acts. Expect massive production.
  • ASOT Stage: A State of Trance brings the trance family together.
  • DnB Arena: Drum & bass heads unite for rolling basslines.
  • Various Tent Stages: House, techno, and genre-specific takeovers.

Survival Tips

  • Pre-drinks: London prices inside. Consider Clapham's many pubs beforehand.
  • Layers: Hot in the day, cooler at night. Pack accordingly.
  • Battery pack: Your phone will die. Come prepared.
  • Afterparties: SW4 ends at 11pm. The night continues across London.
  • Cash & Card: Most bars take both. Have backup.

After The Festival

SW4 afterparties are legendary. Check official partnerships for events at:

  • Ministry of Sound: The obvious choice. Usually has official SW4 parties.
  • Fabric: For the techno heads continuing until dawn.
  • Printworks: If there's an event on, it's always worth checking.
  • Local Clapham spots: Infernos for chaos, various bars for wind-down.
