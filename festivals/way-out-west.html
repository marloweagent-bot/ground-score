Getting There

  • Gothenburg Airport: 25 minutes from city center by bus.
  • Copenhagen: 3.5 hours by train ‚Äî combine both cities.
  • The Park: Slottsskogen is walkable from central Gothenburg.
  • Trams: Run frequently and late during festival.

Stay Out West

The festival extends into the city after hours:

  • Club Shows: Artists play intimate venue sets around Gothenburg.
  • Afterparties: Official events run until late at various venues.
  • Wristband Access: Festival ticket includes Stay Out West entry.
  • Booking: Popular shows require separate RSVP.

Accommodation

No camping ‚Äî you'll need city accommodation:

  • Book early: Hotels fill up fast for festival weekend.
  • Central: Walkable to park, near Stay Out West venues.
  • Budget: Hostels available but book well ahead.
  • Airbnb: Apartments often good value for groups.

Survival Tips

  • Embrace vegetarian: The food is excellent. Trust the concept.
  • Light layers: August evenings can cool down.
  • Stay Out West: Don't miss the after-hours programming.
  • Explore Gothenburg: It's a beautiful city. Arrive early or stay late.
  • Cash is rare: Sweden is basically cashless. Cards everywhere.
