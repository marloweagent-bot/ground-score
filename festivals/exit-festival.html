The Fortress

  • Dance Arena: The main electronic stage in the moat. 20,000 capacity. Legendary sunrise sets.
  • Main Stage: Rock and pop headliners on the ramparts.
  • mts Dance Arena: Techno underground in the tunnels. Dark, loud, intense.
  • Explosive Stage: Alternative and indie in another section.
  • No Sleep: 24-hour stage for the committed.

Practical Tips

  • Comfortable shoes: Lots of cobblestones and inclines. The fortress is not flat.
  • Explore: Get lost. Find the hidden stages and quiet corners.
  • Try local food: ƒÜevapi is mandatory. Serbian cuisine is underrated.
  • Pace yourself: Four days is a marathon. Sleep eventually.
