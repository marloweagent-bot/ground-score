## Scoring Guidelines
- **5.0** = Best in class, exceptional
- **4.0-4.5** = Very good, notable strength
- **3.0-3.5** = Solid, meets expectations
- **2.0-2.5** = Below average, notable weakness
- **1.0-1.5** = Poor, significant issue

## Multi-Genre vs Electronic-Focused Scoring

For **multi-genre festivals** (Lollapalooza, Bonnaroo, etc.):
- Score Sound based on electronic stages specifically (Perry's Stage, The Other, etc.)
- Note the overall festival context but focus on electronic music experience
- Highlight which stages/areas to prioritize for electronic fans
